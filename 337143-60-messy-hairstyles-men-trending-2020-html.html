How do you do a messy hairstyle?

Before we get into the examples of messy men's hairstyles, you first have to understand how the style is done, and what it entails. Usually, a messy look is not one many people would go for. However, for men's hair, there is a difference between having messy hair and being messed up.

Steps of achieving the desired look

The right procedure can help with attaining the much-sought-after messy hairdo. Check out the steps below.

  • Visit a trusted barber. This is where you get to work with him on the length you prefer. If you want shorter hair then you need someone that is good at their work cutting it for you. What's more, the right texture for any messy hairstyle has to be done just right or the entire look gets spoilt.
  • Minimize shampooing your hair. While all hairstyles will become stronger and healthier if you apply this rule, if you are going for the untamed look, you have to be extra vigilant to observe this rule. Shampooing it regularly interferes with the volume of the hair which then disrupts the messy look you are trying to achieve.
  • Blow-dry your hair. This helps control the humidity in it, as opposed to using other techniques. Do not assume that towelling will work. On the contrary, randomly drying your mane with your hands or a towel can only lead to an impossible to manage hairdo.
  • Pick styling products that work for your kind of hair, always bearing in mind that the rugged-look is your end goal. Be careful about using the products in excess. Keep it moderate always.
  • Don't let the hair grow too long. Remember to visit your barber for trims every 4 to 6 weeks if you want to maintain the messy hairdo. It looks better when it is shorter.

    What is a regular haircut called?

    This is a men's and boys' hairstyle with hair that can be combed. It has a defined or deconstructed side part, as well as a short, medium, semi-short, long, or can even be extra long at the back and the sides. It is the perfect haircut to achieve a messy look with.

    What is undercut hair?

    This is a hairdo that rocked between 1910-1940 and was common for men. It resurfaced in the 1980s before becoming very popular in 2010s. It is a classy hairstyle.

    How can a man grow his hair out?

    It is easy to grow one's hair as long as you make regular trips to the barber for trimming, you switch up products, and you exercise patience. You may want to learn the tricks of getting this done especially if you are serious about the messy hairdo.
