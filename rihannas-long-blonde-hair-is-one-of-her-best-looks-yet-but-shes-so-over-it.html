Rihanna's long blonde hair is one of her best looks yet - but she's 'so over it'

Rihanna stepped out with one of her best hairstyles yet this weekend, as she attended a launch event for her Fenty Beauty brand in LA on Friday, but she’s probably ditched it already.

Earlier this year, the music star stepped out in chic blonde locks, and this weekend, she opted for a longer style, matching her stunning gown. However, if there’s one thing about Rihanna, she’s not keeping any hairstyle very long, as she admits she’s “already over” the blonde look. Well, this style was a great send off…

Rihanna stuns with long blonde hair – but she’s already ‘over it’

Taking to the “golden carpet” the mom of two stunned in a sand-colored low-cut dress, showing off all her curves. Matching her hair, and the background with the look, the star posed for pics as she accessorized with, you guessed it, gold jewellery.

The Umbrella singer finished the look off with nude heels, which had laces that wrapped around her legs.

Her blonde hair, which sat below her shoulders was parted to the side, and fans were obsessed with the new look, as they took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to gush.

“She looks amazing with blonde hair!” exclaimed one fan.

“Truly one of the most beautiful women to ever exist,” commented another.

“Rihanna in this blonde hair is stunning,” a third penned.

Unfortunately, though, it doesn’t look like it’s here to stay. Speaking at her event, the 36-year-old told Allure: “I get bored. I get bored, and I need to move on to something, or I’m going to crawl out of my body,” before later adding she was “over it anyway.”

“Tomorrow, I’m not going to have this hairstyle and my hairstylist is going to be mad about it.”

The star continues to fangirl over Bravo

One thing we just learnt about Rihanna, is her love for reality TV, especially Bravo. Who knew the singer was a coach potato just like us?

At her most recent event, she joked she wanted to “stop the interview” as the reporter told her she hadn’t seen any of the shows.

She then went on to give her expert advice, telling the interviewer to start with Vanderpump Rules, and then ‘putting the Housewives series into a hat’ before picking which one to watch.

“I feel like Andy needs to pay me at this point bro!” she jokingly exclaims after her deep analysis of the shows. Cohen, get on it!
