Contagion (2011)

Contagion (2011)

Credit: Amazon

We understand this movie cuts a little too close to home, and it may be a little while until you see this one. This 2011 film details the story of a young woman who returns from Hong Kong with a strange illness, only to die several days later. Spreading at a high rate, characters try to find the source and a cure for this strange illness turned world pandemic.

I Am Legend
