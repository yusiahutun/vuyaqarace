9 celebs who have admitted getting breast surgery: from Chrissy Teigens 3 boob jobs and Dol

Chrissy Teigen has just admitted to having three boob jobs. In an appearance on Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen, the model shared that one of her “lies” around having three boob jobs was actually true. “I’ve had a lift, a put in, a take them out. I don’t like them out,” she said.

Not all celebrities have been as candid as Teigen though. Despite speculation, some continue to deny that they’ve ever gone under the knife.

So, besides Teigen, who else has been open about having work done on their breasts?

2. Iggy Azalea

The Australian rapper admitted to getting breast augmentation surgery in a 2015 interview with E!.

“I love them so much I had to talk about them,” she gushed. “I’d feel better not to have some secret,” she added. “It’s much freer to say it and then you don’t have to worry someone will dig it up.” The same surgeon who worked on her breasts also altered her nose, as reported by People magazine.

3. Dolly Parton

The “Jolene” hitmaker hasn’t been shy about her boob jobs. According to Hello!, she once revealed that she spent a million bucks on breast augmentations. “I just paid a million dollars for them, and I hope they look like a million dollars, too!” she was quoted as saying.

4. Cardi B

The rapper has gone on record saying her enhancements helped her feel more confident. “When I was 18 and became a dancer, I had enough money to afford to buy boobs, so every insecurity that I felt about my breasts was gone,” she told Mariah Carey in an Interview cover story.

5. Kourtney Kardashian

According to People, The Kardashians reality TV star got breast augmentation when she was just 22 years old. “I had my boobs done but if I could go back, I wouldn’t have done it. I was so cute before,” she told Showbiz Spy in 2011. “I’ve realised that I was made to look a certain way and I’m considering removing them.”

6. Heidi Montag

The Hills alum stirred controversy when she underwent a whopping 10 cosmetic procedures in one day in 2009, which included breast augmentation, according to People.

7. Anna Faris

The House Bunny actress said she got breast augmentation surgery because she “wanted to fill out a bikini”.

“I was always a negative-A cup, so when I was 30, I was getting a divorce, I had just finished House Bunny, and I’d sold another movie,” Faris told Women’s Health. “All of these new things were happening to me, so I got my breasts done …[and] it was f***ing awesome.”

8. Victoria Beckham

After denying it for years, Posh finally admitted getting a boob job in an open letter to her younger self for British Vogue in 2017. “Don’t mess with your boobs,” said the Spice Girls singer, “All those years I denied it – stupid. A sign of insecurity. Just celebrate what you’ve got.” Per news.com.au she has since had her implants removed.

9. Kylie Jenner

Pretty much every member of the Kardashian-Jenner household has been accused of undergoing plastic surgery, with many of them eventually admitting to it.
