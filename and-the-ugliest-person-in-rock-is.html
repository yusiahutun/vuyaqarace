And The Ugliest Person In Rock Is

Our culture is obsessed with lists, so this new one should not come as
any surprise.

In Kerrang.com survey's of the ugliest person in rock, Nickelback's Chad Kroeger
takes the honors, followed by Muse's Matt Bellamy and Lemmy of Motorhead.
Kerrang.com's 10 Ugliest People In Rock are:

1 – Chad Kroeger, Nickelback
2 – Matt Bellamy, Muse
3 – Lemmy, Motorhead
4 – Deryck Whibley, Sum 41
5 – Rivers Cuomo, Weezer
6 – Drowning Poll (full band)
7 – Gordon Morrison, Raging Speedhorn
8 – Marilyn Manson
9 – Stuart Cable, Stereophonics
10 – Mortis
