Dana White feels like a 'superhero' and reveals insane body transformation through 86-hour water fas

Dana White is feeling indestructible after finishing an 86-hour water fast.

The UFC president showed off his insane body transformation after already levelling-up his physique in recent years.

White showed off the impressive before and after shots in a two-minute video shared to X, formerly Twitter.

The UFC boss captioned the post: "I did an 86 hour water fast and I feel INCREDIBLE!

"Obviously I'm not a doctor, but I've been told by the experts that water fasting can help to significantly reduce the risk of cancer, alzheimers and a ton of other health issues," though he urged his followers on social media to conduct their own research as he merely pointed out it worked for him.

He added: "I feel like a superhero, I feel incredible, and I want to tell you how I did it."

White revealed that he started fasting after he left UFC 295 on 11 November.

He says he completed the fast on 15 November before showing off the insane transformation.

The 54-year-old went on to reference an online seven-day water fast before claiming that doctors' studies have shown the fast gives an individual an over 70 per cent chance to avoid deadly diseases such as cancer and Alzheimer's.

White added that he sought the guidance of human biologist and bio hacking expert Gary Brecka who advised him throughout the fast.
