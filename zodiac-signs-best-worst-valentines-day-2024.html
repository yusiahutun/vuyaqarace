4 Zodiac Signs Will Have the Best &amp; Worst Valentine's Day 2024

If you’re dreaming of love notes from secret admirers and over-the-top displays of affection, it’s only because Valentine’s Day is just around the corner. And while romance is available to all, certain zodiac signs will have the best Valentine’s Day—while others will have the worst. If your zodiac sign happens to fall under the latter category, don’t worry. The stars always have a say, but V-Day has nothing to do with astrology anyway. You can always make your own magic.

Even though Valentine’s Day is brimming with heart-shaped candy and bright red roses, the holiday can be rather polarizing. While Valentine’s Day is a chance to celebrate your relationship and give your boo some extra lovin’, it often leaves those of us who are living the single life feeling left out. If you’re feeling pressured into pairing up, tune that noise out! Single or not, February 14 is the holiday of love—and the only right way to celebrate it is by spreading love everywhere you go.

This upcoming Valentine’s Day will be one of the most interesting we’ve experienced in years. After all, Mars—planet of passion and sex—will be joining forces with Pluto in Aquarius for the first time in this lifetime. This will intensify our primal instincts and emotions, provoking arguments and plenty of makeup sex. The moon will also be moving through Taurus—the zodiac sign of its exaltation—while joining forces with Jupiter, planet of joy and expansion. This is sure to increase the desire for sensual connection, as well as lead to a big conclusion to something that may have already begun. Is someone ready to admit their feelings for you? Or perhaps that their feelings have changed?

Curious to see if the stars have anything to say about Cupid’s favorite holiday? Read on for what to expect below.

Zodiac Signs Who Will Have the Best Valentine’s Day

  • Taurus

    You’re in the mood to wear your heart on your sleeve during Valentine’s Day 2024. With a moon-Jupiter conjunction rising in your first house of the self, you’re feeling confident, larger-than-life, and ready for something big to happen. Whether you’re single or taken, February 14 is all about tapping into who you are and what you stand for. Even if you’re in a relationship, you may feel like taking time to nurture yourself through self-love and self-care. And if you’re single, this is a great Valentine’s Day to spoil yourself rotten!   • Capricorn

    Get excited, because you’ll be the most attractive person in the room on Valentine’s Day. Venus—planet of love and luxury—is still moving through Capricorn, which enhances your beauty, lovability, and the odds that your flirtatious will be reciprocated. You’ll have no problem finding a date on February 14, if that’s what you’re looking for. And as if that wasn’t enough of a reason to enjoy yourself, keep in mind that the Taurus moon will also be moving through your fifth house of pleasure and fun, urging you to express yourself and explore your romantic side. However, this is also a powerful opportunity to set your priorities straight, especially if you have a lover or a friend you want to express your desires to on Valentine’s Day.   • Pisces

    You might feel more like self-isolating than socializing on Valentine’s Day. After all, the sun, Mercury, and Mars will all be surging through your 12th house of solitude and self-reflection, which could bring up fears and anxieties that make it difficult to connect with others. Remember, Valentine’s Day is just a holiday and it can come and go with no pressure at all. However, if you don’t want to miss out on the heart-shaped festivities, then put your phone on “do not disturb” and plan a rom-com marathon at home or invite a lover over for an extra discreet day of low-key activities on the couch.
