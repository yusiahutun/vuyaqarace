Reese Witherspoon Can't Stop Wearing Swedish Hasbeens

Reese Witherspoon is back in New York City filming a Netflix romantic comedy, doing the most New York thing possible: taking styling tips from the one and only Sarah Jessica Parker. Naturally, that means Witherspoon is now extremely on trend and all over the clog phenomenon that is unexpectedly everywhere and on track to be this fall's breakout It shoe.

SJP has been wearing clogs by the famously comfortable brand, Swedish Hasbeens, for years, and Witherspoon has worn them on repeat this week while on set. First, she was spotted wearing the brand's classic clog, which retails for $260 on Nordstrom. Then, she wore the $290 Sky T-Strap Pump in nature leather. Witherspoon also opted for an open-toe option, and now is truly the perfect time to embrace wearing that kind of sandal with a cardigan, while we still can with this transitional weather.

Shop now: $290; nordstrom.com

The brand is nicely stocked at Nordstrom, which has a wide selection available now, although we wouldn't be surprised if Witherspoon causes a sell-out. Swedish Hasbeens were already famous for being the best, most durable, comfortable-yet-chic clogs on the market. Now, they've been endorsed by not one, but two major style icons. If you don't have a pair, this is the sign you've been waiting for.

Clogs aren't just trendy though, as they're also easy to wear. Witherspoon has styled them in the most '90s way possible, with denim knee-length skirts and green cargo jackets. Sarah Jessica Parker has worn her go-to pairs with denim overalls, fedoras, and ripped purple jeans. They've got range! Not to mention, the shoes are simply far-more attainable than the lug sole Gucci loafer or Prada Monolith boots everyone else is obsessed with.

In conclusion, there's nothing like a fall fit with the perfect shoe inspired by rom com queen herself, Reese Witherspoon. So out with the Hot Girl Summer and in with the Hot Clog Fall. That has a ring to it right?
