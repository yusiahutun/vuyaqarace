Biography

King Krule was born in England on August 24, 1994. Known for music that fuses many different genres, including jazz, punk, hip-hop, new wave, and electronic dance, this British vocalist, songwriter, and guitarist released his debut studio album, Six Feet Beneath the Moon, in 2013. Critics have often compared King Krule’s musical style to that of Scottish vocalist Edwyn Collins. He began recording music in 2010 under the alias Zoo Kid; he would adopt the moniker King Krule a year later.
