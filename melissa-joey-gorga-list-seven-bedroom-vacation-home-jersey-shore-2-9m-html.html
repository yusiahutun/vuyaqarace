Melissa and Joey Gorga list their SEVEN bedroom vacation home on the Jersey Shore for $2.9m.

Real Housewives Of New Jersey star Melissa Gorga and her husband Joey are ready to say goodbye to their lavish home located on the Jersey Shore.

The power couple has listed the sprawling ocean-side retreat that they have been spending their summers enjoying for the past few years for $2.9million.

There is plenty of space at the dwelling located on the Toms River in New Jersey as it has  seven bedrooms and five bathrooms.

In 2010 it was up for sale again at $3.8 million but there were no takers.

Then again in 2017 the home was listed, that time at $3.5 million, before it was de-listed.

And in 2020, it was put back on the market for approximately $3million, before finally being snapped up for two-and-a-half million.
