Buy Noel Fieldings smiley jumper: GBBO hosts quirky episode 2 outfit! Tue 29 September 20

Biscuit Week is well and truly here for a group of competitive bakers on this year’s Bake Off.

While there could be a few disasters in store, it can be guaranteed that host Noel Fielding will bring a smile to episode 2 of the Channel 4 baking show. According to the previews of Great British Bake Off, he will impress with an iconic smiley jumper as bakers are put to the ultimate test against the clock.

So where can viewers get Noel’s smiley jumper for themselves? We found the host’s outfit online for you!

Ep2. Noel and Marc. Picture: Channel 4. Photographer: Mark Bourdillon

Where to buy Noel Fielding’s smiley jumper

Noel is wearing a light yellow jumper made by Belgian fashion designer Raf Simons.

It is called ‘smiley embroidery panelled jumper‘ and it costs £660 for a size small and £1,346 for a extra small, which are the only sizes available on online designer company Far Fetch.

Upon research, it looks like Far Fetch is the only online store that sells the jumper.

Luckily for GBBO viewers, the jumper is currently on sale with 20 percent off. It is also available in black, however this costs more, starting at £880 at 2FS.com.

Similar smiley jumpers from other sites

There are more affordable options for smiley jumpers on other online clothing sites. This includes a blue jumper from Far Afield which has a smaller smiley face on the front, which is on sale for £43.

In other options, you can still make a statement without having to buy the actual jumper that Noel Fielding has.

ASOS is selling a black jumper with multiple yellow smiley faces for £48.

There are also a range of smiley jumpers being sold on eBay. They usually cost between £15 to £35 on average.

Get the T-shirt version of Noel’s smiley jumper

For those who prefer keeping things light, the quirky Raf Simons jumper can be bought in a T-shirt version.

It is less than half the price of the jumper, costing £266 on sale, and is called the ‘smiley print T-shirt’.
