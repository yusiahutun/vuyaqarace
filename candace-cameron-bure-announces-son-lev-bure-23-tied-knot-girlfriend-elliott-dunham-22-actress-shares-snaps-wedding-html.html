Candace Cameron Bure announces son Lev Bure, 23, tied the knot with girlfriend Elliott Dunham, 22 -

Candace Cameron Bure announced her eldest son Lev Bure, 23, tied the knot with girlfriend Elliott Dunham, 22.

The Full House star shared a series of snaps, taken by photographer Michael Derjabin, from the outdoor woodsy wedding that took place over the weekend on Saturday, January 27.

Candace, 47, shared how 'grateful' she is of her 'generational blessing of long lasting Christ-centered marriages' in her family, adding that the Holy Spirit was present at the nuptials.

The actress gushed about the wedding and congratulated her son and now daughter-in-law Elliott, introducing them as Lev and Elliott Bure.

Candace wore a gold textured gown to her son's wedding, opting to wear her blonde locks in in waves around her. 

The actress is married to former NHL player Val Bure, with whom she welcomed three children: Lev, Natasha, 25, and Maksim, 22.

Candace and Val married in 1996. 

Candace opened her slideshow for their wedding on Instagram with: 'I present to you, Mr. and Mrs. Lev Bure. Words cannot express the joy we have felt this weekend celebrating the marriage of our son and his bride Elliott.'

She continued: 'We gained a beautiful daughter and a wonderful family to do life with. I have an overwhelming sense of love, joy, peace and contentment thanks to God's blessing of family and friendship. My heart is so full.' 

'I'm grateful for our generational blessing of long lasting Christ-centered marriages to have a legacy of great-grandparents and grandparents who have shown us the way. And now, being the example for our children and their children to come.

'If this isn't a blessing, I don't know what is. What a celebration it was!! The Holy Spirit was present, the gospel was preached and love filled the air. Covenant vows were made and hearts watching were reminded of theirs long ago.

'We talked, we ate, speeches were given, toasts were made, we laughed, we danced, we celebrated! What a glorious day!!! This mama is over the moon HAPPY!!! Congratulations to Lev and Elliott Bure!!!!'

Lev and Elliott, who graduated May 2023 from Baylor University, got engaged in July 2023.

Lev graduated from Liberty University through an online program with a bachelor's degree in biblical and theological studies.

In 2021, Candace revealed in an interview that Lev works for the family business - Bure Family Wines - a winery in Napa Valley the actress founded with her brother Kirk Cameron in 2006.

Lev's title is Vice President of Bure Family Wines. 

Lev lives in Napa Valley, per Candace, and 'works for our family business full time.'

Lev dated Duck Dynasty star Bella Robertson in 2018, and went to prom together.

Their breakup was revealed in May 2020 and three months later, it was revealed that Lev got engaged to girlfriend Taylor Hutchinson when he was 20.

However, Lev and Taylor split in April 2021 and called off their wedding eight months after he proposed.
