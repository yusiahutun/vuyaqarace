Andy Frisellas Journey From Thousands To Millions

Andy Frisella, an American entrepreneur, is one of many people who have successfully grown their businesses by tapping into the heart of social networking.

Many social media developments have occurred in this era, and those who effectively advertised their brands on social media are now among the wealthiest individuals. Take, for example, the Kardashians. But Frisella’s narrative is really different and very interesting.

Is He Rich Because of Social Media?

“Every day, we’d walk to every surrounding business, shake hands with the owners and employees in the store, and I’d say, ‘Hey, my name is Andy, and I own Supplement Superstores down the road.'”

This is how the entrepreneur describes his marketing strategy prior to the introduction of social media. First, he would welcome individuals and encourage them to sample his goods.

Andy Frisella saw how everyone was uploading photographs of their protein powder on social media, but he claimed in an interview with Forbes that he wanted to do something “different,” so he concentrated on the motivating part rather than the lifestyle.

Frisella performed the same thing he did before, talking to people one on one, but this time he used his personal and brand accounts to engage with the digital public.

As a result, it increased brand and consumer trust.

“We’ve even had folks get our logo tattooed.” That’s the kind of thing that shows us we’re not just selling health products; we’re also creating a space where people feel like they belong.”

Said the 37-year-old entrepreneur, reflecting on how his marketing expertise has aided the brand’s growth.

Surprisingly, they never had to undertake any digital marketing as a result of their strategies, and the CEO of 1st Phorm also disclosed that they did not begin putting their hands in that field until 2017.

Over two decades ago, he and his business partner, Chris, founded Supplement Superstores and then added 1st Phorm and Paradise Distribution for a total of $12,000. By 2015, their company’s revenue had surpassed $100 million, and it was expected to reach $150 million by 2017.
