Travis Kelce is showing Taylor Swift effect after his epic touchdown

They are in love and you can tell! Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift celebrate an epic touchdown of the NFL heartthrob and fans couldn’t get over happy Taylor’s reaction to the significant moment in the Chiefs-Bills game.

Taylor Swift is definitely teaching a thing or two to Travis Kelce behind the scenes as the NFL icon made a signature heart sign right after his touchdown. The crowd went wild and a certain music legend celebrated the moment with her celebrity friends.

Taylor Swift makes a sneaky heart after Travis Kelce’s love move

Travis Kelce‘s touchdown made plenty of fans happy and Taylor Swift happier than ever in any game so far this season. The 34-year-old looked ecstatic as Travis Kelce made a heart shape towards the VIP stand.

She returned the sweet gesture by making a quick heart, as Brittany Mahomes came to embrace the singer in a celebratory hug. Later, Tay didn’t forget to blow a kiss to her beau, which melted fan hearts. Along with Tay Tay, Trav’s mother Donna Kelce, father Ed Kelce and older brother Jason Kelce joined in the celebration. Jason even took his shirt off at the final whistle while cheering for Travis.

The Taylor Swift effect

Travis Kelce is famous for doing a signature shooting arrow sign after every touchdown and fans are witnessing the Taylor Swift effect in real time, as he switched to making a heart after his latest feat.

Excited fans took to X (formerly Twitter) to celebrate Travis’s heart-swooning move. One posted: “Omg Travis Kelce just did heart hands at Taylor Swift after his touchdown, football may never recover!”

Another echoed the feelings saying: “Travis Kelce giving Taylor Swift the fearless hand heart to celebrate his touchdown is the best thing that’s happened to society in years.”

A third fan posted: “Travis Kelce making a heart with his hands up to Taylor Swift after scoring a TD just warmed my cold dead heart. Jason Kelce going absolutely berserk screaming was just fantastic.”
