Lil Yachty biography

McCollum A.K.A Lil Yachty was born in Mableton, Atalanta, in the USA on August 23, 1997. He grew up listening to Drake, Soulja Boy, Kanye West, and Coldplay. While going to Pebblebrook Highschool, Lil Yachty also worked at McDonald’s. It is here that he met and interacted with other aspiring rappers and producers.

Yachty went to Alabama State University in 2015 but dropped out to follow his passion for music. After leaving Alabama State University, Yachty moved from Atalanta to New York and adopted the name Lil Yachty. During the summer, he spent most of his time in the Big Apple hoping to meet and establish connections with people who would help him launch his career.

At this time, he was staying with a friend and used to network with online street fashion personalities. He used that opportunity to build his own Instagram. Yachty and his friend were arrested in September 2015 at a Palm Beach Gardens mall in Florida for credit fraud. The rapper was released after posting a bail bond of $11000.

Career highlights

The very first song Yachty ever released was “One Night”, and it did very well thanks to his unique style. He gained prominence almost instantly, and he did not waste time working on other tracks. A year later, his debut mixtape titled Lil Boat was already out peaking at number 106 in the US Billboard chart.

Before his breakthrough in the music industry, Yachty had the chance to model at Kanye West’s Yeezy Season 3 fashion show. His second mixtape, Summer Song 2, was also released in 2016, followed by a third mixtape, Lil Boat 2.

Lil Yachty’s studio debut album "Teenage Emotions" was released in May of 2017. The album debuted at number 5 on the US Billboard 200 with hit singles such as Bring it Back, Harley, and Peek a Boo. Although he is yet to win any significant award, Lil Yachty has received nominations for the MTV Europe Music Award and Grammy Award.
