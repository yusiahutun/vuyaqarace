Furthermore, Allen made her appearance in the movie, The Best Man and Blue’s Big Musical Movie released during the period 1999s. She also played a small role in a Sesame Street direct-to-video special, Elmo’s Magic Cookbook (2001). Later, she starred in a blockbuster comedy film, School Of Rock, which made her famous in the acting sector.
