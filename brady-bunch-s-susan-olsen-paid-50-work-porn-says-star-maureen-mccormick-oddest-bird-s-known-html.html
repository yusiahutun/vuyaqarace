Susan Olsen worked in porn for $50 and says Maureen McCormick is an 'odd bird'

She's known for playing wholesome schoolgirl Cindy Brady in the popular 1970s American TV show, The Brady Bunch.

But Susan Olsen, now 53, has opened up about her work in the porn industry.

Speaking to WS FM101.7's Jonesy & Amanda on Monday morning, the actress, who portrayed the youngest Brady child from 1969 until 1974, explained that the rumour she got into porn movies is true - and it was all because she owned a synthesiser.
