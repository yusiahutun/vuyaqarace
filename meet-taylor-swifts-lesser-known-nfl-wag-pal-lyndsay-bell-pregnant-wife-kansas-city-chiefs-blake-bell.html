Meet Taylor Swifts lesser-known NFL WAG pal, Lyndsay Bell: the pregnant wife of Kansas City

Wait – who’s Lyndsay Bell, you ask? In case you haven’t noticed, Swift has been snapped with the wife of Kansas City Chiefs American football tight end Blake Bell in recent months, though they have remained low-key about their friendship.

Either way, she’s become a solid addition to Swift’s A-list girl gang. Here’s what you need to know about the Eras Tour billionaire’s lesser-known gal pal.

Who is Lyndsay Bell married to?

Lyndsay Bell is married to American football player Blake Bell.

As reported by Us Weekly, the lovebirds met at university, before Blake became a professional athlete.

She shares a daughter with her hubby – and is pregnant with their second child

The Bells got married in February 2021 and welcomed their first child, daughter Brinleigh, the following year, according to Us Weekly.

Lyndsay recently confirmed her second pregnancy, posing with her baby bump in a Chiefs VIP suite with her two friends, Swift and Mahomes.

She’s very private

Lyndsay has remained very private about her personal life. In fact, her Instagram account is private, so fans of the WAG can only get glimpses into her life via her husband’s account – or, of course, Swift’s and Mahomes’, as well as some of her other friends’ public profiles.

She’s a Swiftie, of course

Even before they became friends, Lyndsay was already a Swiftie. In a recent interview with reporters in Las Vegas, her husband confirmed that his wife is a fan of the “Anti-Hero” songstress, per Heavy.

“She’s been blessed to have great friends on the team,” he added. “The wives have been so kind to her. They have their little crew and she’s enjoying it.

“I always joke around (with Lyndsay),” he noted. “‘You’re getting a little bit more pub than I am!’”

She regularly hangs out with Taylor Swift and Brittany Mahomes

Lyndsay has been out and about with the girls on numerous occasions. According to Heavy, as well as many Swift fan pages and Mahomes’ IG, Swift has invited Lyndsay to her Manhattan home, and they’ve also gone on several GNOs together.
