Who's Allison From The Bachelor 2024? Joey Spoilers, Job, Instagram

She may have her sister, Lauren, but Season 28 is a solo journey for Allison on The Bachelor 2024. So who is Allison from The Bachelor? Keep reading for what we know about Allison from The Bachelor Season 28 and what happens with her and Joey.

Allison Hollinger is one of 32 contestants on The Bachelor Season 28 with Joey Graziadei. Joey was a contestant on The Bachelorette Season 20 with Charity Lawson, where he was the runner-up. He was announced as the Season 28 Bachelor at The Bachelorette Season 20 “After the Final Rose” special in August 2023. “I’m looking for someone that’s just themselves. Someone that challenges me. Someone to do life with,” Joey told host Jesse Palmer during the “After the Final Rose” special.

In an interview with BachelorNation.com in January 2024, Joey revealed whether he had any regrets about his season as the Bachelor. “I’m not really a person to have regrets. What I tried to do the whole time and what I continue to try to do is be myself and lead with what I think is the best decision,” he said. “We all make mistakes and I’m not going to look back on anything and say that was something I regret. I think that I did this to the best of my ability. I know that, so I can’t really live with any regrets with that being the case.”

As for why he wanted to become the Bachelor, Joey told the website that he realized the process worked after he almost found The One with Charity. “It’s the fact that I’ve been through it and I know what the show can bring out,” he said. “One of the beautiful parts of it is that you are pushed to be your best self. You are in an uncomfortable situation, but how you deal with that says a lot about a person. It creates an atmosphere and environment that I believe in. I think it’s a great way to get to know someone.”

He continued, “Sometimes in dating, it takes a while to learn about someone to really figure out if you are compatible. There is often a lot of fluff in the beginning and you don’t have time for that in this and I think it makes it more real. You get to know someone to their core very quickly and that’s a really important thing for a healthy relationship.”

But back to Allison from The Bachelor 2024. So who is Allison from The Bachelor Season 28 and does she end up with Joey? Read on for what happens to Allison from The Bachelor 2024 and where she is now. (For more Bachelor 2024 spoilers, including who wins Joey’s season, click here.)

  • Who Is Allison from The Bachelor 2024?

    Who is Allison from The Bachelor 2024? Allison Hollinger is a 26-year-old Realtor from Dover, Delaware. Her Instagram handle is @allisonhollinger. “Here we go…tune in Jan 22nd🌹,” she captioned an Instagram post in December 2023 announcing herself as a contestant on The Bachelor Season 28. Her sister, Lauren, commented, “OR DONT ..thats okay too.”

    Read Allison’s official Bachelor biography and fun facts:

    “Allison is ready for her rom-com level love story. The real estate agent has had multiple serious relationships but still hasn’t found Mr. Right. She’s looking for a man who is loyal, positive, mature and kind, so she’s excited to see if Joey is her perfect match. There’s one catch though … Allison will be looking for love alongside her older sister and best friend, Lauren. The two have dated the same guy before (at different times), so let’s hope that experience prepared them for “The Bachelor.” Will Allison get the fairy-tale ending that she has always wanted with Joey? Only time will tell!

    Fun Facts:
    • Allison has won many costume contests.

  • What Does Allison Do For a Living From The Bachelor 2024?

    What does Allison do for a living from The Bachelor 2024? Allison listed her job on The Bachelor Season 28 as a “Realtor.” Her older sister, Lauren, for her part, listed her job on The Bachelor Season 28 as a “Registered Nurse.” According to her Linkedin, Lauren works as an “Aesthetic Nurse Injector.” She graduated from West Virginia University in 2017 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology. She also earned her Associate’s Degree in Registered Nursing from Montgomery College in 2021.

  • Do Allison and Joey End Up Together on The Bachelor 2024?

    Do Allison and Joey end up together on The Bachelor 2024? The answer is no. Allison went home in Week 4 on The Bachelor Season 28 and was not the winner of Joey’s season, according to Reality Steve.

    Before her elimination, Allison went on no One-on-One dates and three Group Dates on The Bachelor Season 28. Allison went home in Week 4 along with Sydney Gordon and Edwina Dorbor. Sydney went home on a Two-on-One Date with Maria Georgas, while Edwina was eliminated at the Rose Ceremony with Allison.

    Allison’s older sister, Lauren, for her part, Lauren self-eliminated during the cocktail party in Week 2 because Allison liked Joey more and she didn’t like him as much, according to Reality Steve. Erika Cardenas, Marlena Haddad, and Taylor Weins also went home that week. Erika, Marlena, and Taylor all went home at the Rose Ceremony. Before her elimination, Lauren went on no One-on-One Dates and one Group Date on The Bachelor Season 28.
