Peter Cincotti Duets with Katie Couric on Today Show

Nineteen-year-old jazz wunderkind Peter Cincotti shared the keyboard with Today Show co-host Katie Couric on June 5 when the two teamed up for a classical duet.
Ramsey Lewis also performed a jazz duet with Cincotti as they launched Steinway & Son's 150th Anniversary Celebration.

Cincotti's self-titled debut CD (Concord Records) has hit #1 on Billboard's Jazz Chart.
