15 richest architects in the world and their net worth 2021

Architecture remains the most influential profession, especially in safeguarding human history. The rapid urbanisation and need to create better cities has created a new class of the richest architects scattered across the world. Interestingly, most of them have started design firms giving more talented designers a chance to get into the rich club.

Who is the richest architect in the world? For close to ten years, Norman Foster has been the wealthiest architect. However, his spot is not guaranteed, especially considering how much his juniors are charging to design modern monuments.

15 richest architects in the world

Architecture can instinctively make you turn left or right when entering a space or building. The brains behind the magnificent structures are worth millions of dollars. Here are the 15 richest architects and their net worth:

1. Norman Foster - $240 million

Norman is the wealthiest architect of all time. Norman Foster net worth of $240 million (£170 million) is mainly from his high-budget projects in Europe and the US. For example, Foster + Partners designed the Citic Bank Headquarters (in Hangzhou, China) and Swiss Tower RE (in London, England).

2. Frank Gehry - $100 million

Frank Gehry is the oldest architect still practising. The Canadian-American architect has also redefined modern cityscapes, especially in North America, thanks to his unique approach to design. His projects, such as the Walt Disney Concert Hall in Los Angeles, Guggenheim Museum in Bilbao, and the Experience Music Project in Seattle, have redefined modern design.

The 92-year-old, as of 2021, has an estimated net worth of $100 million. Frank has accumulated his wealth mainly from his seven decades of design in different countries, mainly in Europe and North America.
