Table of Biography

Zedd is a famous German artist, DJ, songwriter, and record producer. Zedd is also famous for his collaboration with numerous artists over the years in the music industry including Foxes, Katy Perry, Selena Gomez, Shawn Mendes, Alessia Cara, Maren Morris and Grey, and Elley Duhé among many other artists.

Early Life

Zedd was born on 2 September 1989 and he is currently 34 years old. He is originally from Saratov, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union (present-day Russia), and his zodiac sign is Virgo. Likewise, his real name is Anton Zaslavski and it is written in Russian as Антон Заславский. Moreover, his nationality is German.

Career

Professionally, Zedd is a DJ, record producer, as well as songwriter. He started his musical journey in Kaiserslautern, Germany, where he grew up.

In the year 2012, this DJ released Clarity which featured Foxes. This propelled Zedd to mainstream success and reached #8 on the Billboard Hot 100. Moreover, this song also earned him a Grammy for Best Dance Recording at the 56th Grammy Awards.

Likewise, the same year, he also released his debut studio album Clarity. This album also includes the hit single Stay the Night which features Hayley Williams. Similarly, in the year 2015, this personality released his 2nd studio album True Colors.

The lead single of this album is I Want You to Know and it features Selena Gomez. Likewise, his song Stay featuring Alessia Cara peaked at #7. Similarly, another single The Middle featuring Maren Morris and Grey peaked at #4.

Moreover, he was a featured artist in the song Break Free by Ariana Grande and it also peaked at #4 in the US. This personality has also collaborated with other popular artists including Katy Perry, Shawn Mendes, Alessia Cara, and Elley Duhé among others.
