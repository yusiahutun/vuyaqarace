Who is Astro:

Astro is a famous Rapper. He was born on June 24, 1957 and his birthplace is England. Astro is also well known as, Rapper known for being a founding member of the ultra-popular reggae group UB40, who has sold more than 70 million records. With the band, he has recorded chart-topping singles like “Red Red Wine” and “Can’t Help Falling in Love.”

Astro is originated from England. He and singer Ali Campbell are founding members of UB40.
