Joe Jonas develops defined abs and muscles after 16 weeks

He recently impressed his fans with rock hard abs after stripping down to his underwear for Guess' Spring 2017 campaign.

And Joe Jonas has Unbreakable Performance Center in West Hollywood, California to thank for that as co-founder Jay Glazer recently revealed the star's workout regime.

The 27-year-old singer trained for 16 weeks in high-intensity and high-power workouts, according to TMZ. 

He's ripped! Joe Jonas underwent 16 weeks of high intense training at Unbreakable Performance Center; here he's pictured with WBC Boxing Champion Ava Knight Salicka

The NFL On Fox star told the outlet that the DNCE frontman jumped back and forth between bulking up then shredding weight with strength training and boxing exercises.

He also shared that Joe worked out twice a day, eating a caloric intake that allowed a particular number of carbs, fat and protein for specific times of the day.

'His workouts were pretty intense,' Jay continued. '[Boxing] shreds his shoulders and his core — it’s one of the best things you could do to shred. The way you really shred up is by getting your heart rate to go up and down drastically.'
