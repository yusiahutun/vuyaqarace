She's known for her laid back, eclectic fashion sense.

And Vanessa Hudgens has taken hipster casual to the next level by going shoeless while out on a coffee run in Los Angeles on Saturday.

The 25-year-old teamed her barefoot chic with a white tank top that had the phrase 'make art not war' across it and a pair of overly long flare bottom pants as she satiated her latest caffeine fix.

Personal style: Vanessa Hudgens showed off her bohemian chic while on a coffee run in Los Angeles on Saturday

The Spring Breakers star accessorized her look with several necklaces as she stepped gingerly on the sidewalk outside a Starbucks.

She wore her highlighted locks up in a bun with no makeup and shielded her eyes with a pair of dark sunglasses.

Vanessa sipped on a large iced beverage while accompanied by a female pal who wore flip flops.

Coffee break: She wore her hair up in a bun with no makeup and dark sunglasses

Peace: Her tank top had the phrase 'make art not war!' written across it

Quench her thirst: The 25-year-old sipped on a large iced beverage

Just yesterday, the High School Musical beauty was spotted out getting her daily caffeine buzz while heading to a studio with friends.

She had her hair up in a bun and wore a short sleeve collared shirt.

Earlier in the week she shared a photo wearing a bright outfit while laying on a hammock.

Getting a buzz: On Friday she stepped out wearing a short sleeve collared shirt to get her coffee

Time off: The actress recently wrapped filming her latest movie Kitchen Sink

She included the message:'It officially feels like summer in LA! Feeling good today in my @Bongojeans tropical prints. #ThunderTropic.'

Vanessa is currently on a break from filming, after recently wrapping up the film Kitchen Sink.

Scheduled to be released next year, the movie is about a small town occupied with humans, vampires and zombies and the three teenagers working to make sure they all get along.
