Model speaks out after video of her getting groped by drunk guy in bar goes viral

Hands said she was looking straight ahead watching her friend and trying to ignore him when things got physical the first time.

'He goes in behind, gets his hand and tries to slap my a** and he’s so drunk that he does it really obviously, so I obviously stop him and go "what are you doing?" I was like "stop" – literally talking to a seven-year-old.'
