He attended the University of Wisconsinâ€“Madison where he played football as running back for the Wisconsin Badgers, Rose Bowl Champions in 1994. With the Chargers, Terrell rushed for 1,871 yards and gained 1,943 yards receiving, leading all Charger running backs in receptions for five consecutive seasons, from 1996-2000.
