Taylor Armstrong, 42, has said 'I do' with lawyer John Bluher, 56, People magazine is reporting.

The couple exchanged vows at the Bel-Air Bay Club in Los Angeles' posh Pacific Palisades, and was witnessed by 120 guests including her former Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills co-stars including Kim and Kyle Richards, Adrienne Maloof and Lisa Vanderpump.

Taylor wore a ivory Chagoury Couture gown and Jimmy Choo shoes, and a sparkling headband adorned her curling mass of blonde hair.

Exchanged vows: Taylor Armstrong exchanged vows last week with lawyer John Bluher on a cliff overlooking the Pacific Ocean in Los Angeles during a lavish ceremony reportedly paid for by a television production company

She carried a bridal bouquet of pink blooms and greenery and was escorted down the aisle by her seven-year-old daughter Kennedy, who wore a satiny ivory frock and headband just like her mother's.

'Today, John, Kennedy and I became a family,' Taylor told People. 'I married my best friend and Kennedy's family circle became complete.'

Bluher wore a black Hugo Boss tuxedo with white Brioni tie and shoes by Prada and presented his bride with a stunning white gold and diamond, double band ring.

The bride wore ivory: For her second trip down the aisle, the former reality star donned a stunning ivory gown by Chagoury Couture and Jimmy Choo shoes

Bound: Taylor held onto John whom she described as her best friend

We are family: Taylor's seven-year-old daughter Kennedy walked her down the aisle and mirrored her look in a shiny ivory dress and sparkling headband

Sumptuous: The wedding was Taylor's vision realised with the help of wedding planner David Tutera

So happy: The radiant bride shared a laugh with her daughter and John during the ceremony that was also captured on videotape

Gorgeous details: The back of Taylor's wedding gown featured an intricate design of lace and crystal beadwork

As People reported, Ari Soffer designed Taylor's wedding and engagement ring and also Bluher's white gold band set with 60 black diamonds.

The sunshiny outdoor celebration was put into motion with the help of event planner David Tutera, and there was music, dancing, food and a salted caramel-frosted, chocolate cake by Sweet E's.

'Taylor had a clear vision of the design,' Tutera told People. 'I took her ideas of Colorado meets Beverly Hills and set it on fire with tons of floral, candlelight and dripping chandeliers.'

Cameras rolling: Kyle Richards looked on as Taylor stood pretty carrying her bountiful bouquet of pink and purple blooms as a camera filmed away

Sunny daze: Kyle Richards was dressed in black that seemed to attract the heat of the sun

The ring: John slipped a stunning white gold ring set with a total of 64 diamonds on Taylor's left hand

It's love: Taylor kissed her handsome groom and flashed an amazing collection of jewels

It was a dream come for Taylor, who endured more than her share of heartbreak before Bluher came into her life.

Taylor was previously wed to Russell Armstrong, who tragically committed suicide by hanging himself in a friend's apartment in 2011 after she filed for divorce amid claims of domestic abuse. 

She grew close to Bluher when he was helping her sort through the legal side of things after Russell's death.

Husband and wife: Taylor made a move to embrace her new groom

Cheers: The newlywed couple toasted to many years of happiness

'John loves me for exactly who I am. I just know there will never be enough days with him,' Taylor gushed.

Taylor signalled that nuptials were in the works via her Twitter page a week ago.

'Wedding is on! @davidtutera has his hands full this time. #love #marriage #celebration,' she giddily informed her 500,000-plus followers.
