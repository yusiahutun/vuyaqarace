Zendaya Exudes Serious &quot;Rich Mom&quot; Energy in Opulent Tennis Whites

At this point, our country club fashion mood boards are just photos of Zendaya from her Challengers press tour—and we are not complaining. For her latest tennis-inspired outfit, she exuded serious “rich mom” energy—specifically, what we imagine an affluent mother might wear to dinner following an intense doubles match.

Arnold Jerocki//Getty Images

The Euphoria star was the epitome of sophistication, dressed in opulent tennis whites today at the Rolex Monte-Carlo Masters tennis tournament in France. With the help of longtime stylist Law Roach, Zendaya started things off cozy with a bright white cable knit sweater that featured a button-up collar, a black-and-yellow emblem on the chest, and wide ribbed cuffs and a matching hemline from Brunello Cucinelli's Fall/Winter 2024 ready-to-wear collection. She elevated the sweater with an elegant creamy-white maxi skirt that featured spacious pockets along each hip and a thigh-high leg slit, from the same collection. She kept things classic with a pair of champagne-colored pointed-toe stilettos and small diamond stud earrings by Bulgari. Even her hair had a quiet luxury feel, as it was styled in bouncy curls behind her with a deep side part. As for makeup, she glammed up with a dewy, glowy base and glossy nude-pink lips.

Zendaya and Roach’s “method dressing” technique has been nothing short of masterful. In the past couple weeks, the “All for Love” singer has channeled her Challengers character in a Thom Brown maxi halter dress that featured a subtle tennis racket pattern throughout and a pleated skirt, a custom-made icy-white Calvin Klein skirt suit, a glittery green gown emblazoned with an image of a figure striking a tennis ball, an electric silver-sequined minidress and tennis-ball-skewered stiletto heels, a gauzy white gown, and a white-and-green checkered suit dress.
