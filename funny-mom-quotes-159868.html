Funny Mom Quotes That Prove It's the Hardest Job out There

Being a mom is no easy feat, and it takes a healthy dose of a good sense of humor and venting to your friends to make it through the week — funny quotes can help, too! Between laundry, dishes, cooking, and making sure everyone’s homework gets done — plus a job that actually pays you if you’re a working mom — there just doesn’t seem to be enough hours in the day sometimes. And if you don’t have a partner to share the burden (or if your partner just doesn’t share the burden that well), it gets even harder. But do you know what helps? A good chuckle. Sometimes, all you need to do is laugh.

We put together some sayings that every mom can relate to. Because we’ve all been there, whether it was sneaking away to eat a bag of chips or keeping an emergency bottle of wine chilling in the fridge at all times. Being a mom is simply one of the most important jobs anybody can have. But sometimes, you just need a little stress relief.
