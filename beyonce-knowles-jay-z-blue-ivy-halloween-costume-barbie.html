Beyonce &amp; Jay Z Dress as Barbie &amp; Ken for Halloween -- See Blue Ivy Too!

Hip hop's first family got all dolled up in NYC on Monday for Halloween.

Beyonce Knowles, Jay Z and Blue Ivy all dressed in Barbie-inspired costumes for the holiday ... and they absolutely crushed their looks.

Queen Bey was dressed as the original doll from 1959, rocking a retro black and white swimsuit, vintage cat eye sunglasses and black peep-toe pumps. She had her hair styled in a chic high pony and completed her look with a sultry red pout.

Hova looked dapper in a black tuxedo, paired with a silver cummerbund and bow tie as Barbie's main man, Ken. The "Holy Grail" rapper completed his look with an impeccably coiffed hairdo.

The duo's adorable daughter was also dressed in head-to-toe in Mattel threads, sporting a sequin skirt, leather jacket covered in Barbie references and colorful, custom-designed kicks from POP Shoes. She held a microphone in her hand as she posed for photos with her famous parents.
