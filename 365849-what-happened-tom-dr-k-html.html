What happened to Tom on Dr. K?

Tom Cronenwett was among the frequent faces on the reality documentary titled Dr. K’s Exotic Animal ER. The American TV show centered around the services of Dr. Susan Kelleher, the lead veterinarian. The facility offers emergency services to exotic animals; all these covered in a thrilling documentary. Of late, however, one of the show’s popular faces has been missing, leading many to ask, what happened to Tom on Dr. K?

The documentary is aired on Nat Geo Wild and is among the most entertaining reality shows. Its first episode was aired in October 2014 and has managed to amass a massive following ever since. Currently, at its eighth season, the program has achieved great success. But as expected, it has its fair share of controversies.

It is common to find new characters joining a particular show and others leaving at the same time. However, the show almost reached the cancellation level once after several former Dr. K Exotic Animal ER staff came up with several allegations against the project. One departure that was the least expected was that of a veterinary technician, Tom Cronenwett.
