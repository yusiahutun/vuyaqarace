Laine Hardy Announces North American Tour

(CelebrityAccess) — Hollywood Records/Industrial Media’s 19 Recordings artist Laine Hardy announced he will be hitting the road for his first headlining North American tour later this year.

Hardy, who won the most recent season of ABC’s American Idol, will kick the tour off on Nov. 14th at The Rave II in Milwaukee, and wrap the run up with a performance at The Barn at Cowboy Jacks in Minneapolis on December 7th.

Hardy, who hails from Livingston, Louisiana is also scheduled to perform at a ‘homecoming show’ on September 28 at North Park in Denham Springs, Louisiana.

As well, he’s lined up for a pair of pre-tour performances with Toby Keith October 5th in Charleston, South Carolina, and with Jimmie Allen October 24th in Nashville, Tennessee.

Hardy just wrapped a performance this past weekend at the KABOO festival, as part of a lineup that included Kings of Leon, Dave Matthews Band, Maren Morris, and Mumford & Sons, among numerous others.

In addition, Hardy has been in the studio in Nashville, writing and preparing to release new music in the near future.
