Nikita Jasmine claims she makes Celebs Go Dating history in explosive new E4 series

Married At First Sight UK bombshell Nikita Jasmine is on the look out for love once again.

The 26-year-old Geordie beauty has signed up to Celebs Go Dating in the hope of finding herself Mr Right.

However, it appears that she gets more than what she bargains for on her latest quest for romance.

Speaking to Daily Star, Nikita said: "There's a couple of unexpected things which have happened to me which has never happened in the Celebs Go Dating history I believe.

"It wasn't done purposely put it that way."

The starlet remained tight-lipped on her history making moment and went on to explain why she joined Anna Richardson and Paul C Brunson's dating agency.

She said: "I've got a lot of love to give.

"I had a bit of a dry patch and its something exciting and different.

"I don't ever go on multiple dates. I've never gone on a date with someone I don't know.

"The blind date situation made me in the first episode made me nervous and I'm not even a nervous person but you just didn't know what to expect."

Nikita has been reunited with love guru Paul for the E4 dating show after previously both appearing on Married At First Sight

During the first episode, which aired on Monday night, Paul cringed as Nikita "threw him under a bus" while on a date.

She told her love interest he'd "got it wrong" on MAFS, saying: "I mean, he was a good looking lad but personality wise, he was just dead.

"He had no banter at all! We had loads of rows, we disagreed on stuff, it was a bit of a shambles.

"Obviously I worked with Paul, he was the love expert on Married At First Sight, he matched me with my husband and he never listened to what I asked for!"

Other celebs hoping to find romance are TOWIE's Chloe Brockett, 5ive band member Abz Love, The Apprentice star Ryan Mark, TV personality Ulrika Jonsson and Made in Chelsea hunk Miles Nazaire.

Geordie Shore's Marty McKenna and Married At First Sight Australia beauty Jessika Power join the agency later in the series.

Celebs Go Dating airs weeknights on E4 at 9pm
