James Milton Trotter Jr. | Death Notices

James Milton Trotter Jr., 55, of New Orleans, and formerly of of Heidelberg, passed away Saturday, Oct. 15, 2022 at his residence. A funeral services will be at 10 a.m. Saturday, Oct. 22, at Shady Grove Missionary Baptist Church in Heidelberg. Burial will be in Shady Grove Church cemetery. Viewing will be from 9-10 a.m. at the church. Chapel of Angels Funeral Home of Laurel is in charge of arrangements. View an online guestbook at chapelofangelsfh.net.
