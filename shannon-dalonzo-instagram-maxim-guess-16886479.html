Maxim model reveals THE diet secret that keeps her in shape: Im feeling great

Maxim and GUESS model Shannon Dalonzo is no stranger to showing off her enviable curves online.

The brunette bombshell is known for her work on various modelling campaigns, as well as her contribution to fitness and sport.

In an exclusive interview with Daily Star Online, Shannon reveals everything about her workout regime.

Shannon, who is currently based in LA and has 110,000 Instagram followers, said: “I’m not going to lie, I do find it hard to workout on days I’m working.

“I like to workout first thing in the morning, so if I have an early call time and don’t fit it in before, it’s tough to do it afterwards.

“I try not to be too hard on myself if I don’t fit it in, but having a personal trainer has helped me a lot.

“I train about 4-5 times a week, but it depends on how busy my work schedule is.

“And my favourite cardio workout is Soul Cycle (based in the US), it’s quick, intense and fun.”

Instagram

Since she was casted as a model for an international GUESS campaign, Shannon has been travelling the world and creating a presence on social media.

Being constantly on the move can make it difficult to stick to a strict diet regime. So what are her top nutrition secrets?

She added: “I went through a couple of different diet phases trying to figure out what worked best for me.

“I base my diet first on how I feel and I always find the results come with it.

“I was vegan for a while and I loved it. I am currently pescatarian and feeling great.”

As with any hectic job, finding the time to exercise can be difficult, but Shannon claims her industry has kept her in shape.

“It’s part of the job. It goes beyond that for me though,” she added.

“When I haven’t worked out for a couple of days, my body just needs to move. So it’s definitely a lifestyle.”

Shannon continued: “I’ve always been an active person, having grown up doing sports, dance and competitive cheerleading that it’s become second nature.

“I don’t foresee myself stopping because it’s what feels normal.

“I actually have been thinking about taking dance back up again and probably will soon.”
