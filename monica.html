What is Monica Age and Net Worth 2023? Details on Her Husband and Kids; Songs

Monica is an American singer and songwriter who is known for releasing her debut album, Miss Thang after signing with Rowdy Records in 1993. Her highest-selling album are The Boy Is Mine, After the Storm, The Makings of Me, and Still Standing.

Her song, The Boy Is Mine made her win the nomination of Best R&B Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals at Grammy Award. Monica’s hits songs are Before You Walk Out of My Life, Don’t Take It Personally, Like This and Like That, etc.

Early Life of Monica

Monica was born Monica Denise Arnold on October 24, 1980, in College Park, Georgia, USA. As of now, Monica is 42 years old. Monica cuts her birthday cake on October 24 and her sun sign is Scorpio. Her parents are M. C. Arnold (father) and Marilyn Best (mother). Her mother is a Delta Air Lines customer service representative while her father worked as a mechanic for an Atlanta freight company.

After her parent’s divorce, her mother married Reverend Edward Best, a Methodist minister. She has a biological sibling and three half-siblings named Montez Arnold, Jermond Grant Arnold, Tron Best, and Cypress Best respectively.

Her paternal grandparents are M. C. Arnold and Maude R. Thomas. Her cousin is Polow da Don, a record producer. She holds American citizenship and is of African-American ethnicity. Monica is a believer in Christianity religion. She studied at North Clayton High School.

Her Marriage With Shannon Brown

She is single but she was previously married to Shannon Brown, an NBA player. In June 2010, they met and started dating each other. Shannon proposed to her with a rose-cut diamond ring on November 22, 2010. They secretly married at their Los Angeles home and announced their wedding on January 21, 2011.

They again got married in the presence of their friends and family in July 2011. Monica filed for divorce in March 2019 and officially divorced in October 2019. After their divorce, none of them receive the alimony and Monica received the full custody of her children.

Monica Opens Up About Endometriosis

She suffered from endometriosis and had an 8-hour of surgery on 30th May 2017. Monica had 2 cysts, fibroids & a hernia which made her sick, and had surgery with Dr. Ceana Nezhat & Northside Hospital Staff. She revealed her endometriosis to People saying:

“My reason for sharing is because we, as women, are built to be warriors and we will ignore something that seems so simple that can be so complex. Your uterine health is very important. I spoke up about it so people out there who are going through the same thing know that they’re not alone.”
