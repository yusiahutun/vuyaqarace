Top 10 apps like Clapper for messaging and live streaming

The advent of short-form video content applications has led to the entry of new platforms like Clapper into the market. Launched in 2020, Clapper became the new contender in town, offering content creators an alternative form of organic video streaming. To that note, new emerging low-budget apps like Clapper are competing with and eating into the share market of multinational giants such as TikTok.

Clapper is a leading and fastest-growing social media platform focusing on interconnecting communities. The platform also provides influencers and content creators a medium to showcase their talents and what they excel in.

Top 10 apps like Clapper

Is Clapper safer than TikTok? Yes, Clapper is a safer video application than TikTok owing to its adherence to users' data privacy. However, despite its excellent data policy safety, the platform has constantly been marred by bugs and downtime. Below are the best apps like Clapper according to Slash Dot:

1. Tik-Tok

TikTok is by far the most popular video streaming and entertainment platform available. The app appeals to content creators owing to its vast global reach and huge database. TikTok also allows content creators to showcase themselves through authentic video uploads based on any topic.

Users and content creators alike can build a fan base by sharing content in the form of short video reels. The app's vast community constantly feeds it with an endless stream of content, hence its appeal as a source of entertainment across users of all ages.

TikTok is a free and easy-to-use platform that offers monetisation benefits to content creators. The app is also compatible with both Android and iOS devices. 

2. Fanbase

Fanbase is one of the growing list of content creation platforms alternatives to Clapper available on the market. Fanbase pips most of its competitors, such as Tik-Tok, owing to its subscription module, which enables content creators to earn income right after joining the platform.

The app charges a monthly subscription of $4.99 per month, and content creators, in turn, earn 50% of all their earnings in return. Fanbase allows content creators to interact with their only fans across mediums such as live streaming, photos and video content.

Fanbase also monetises through organic means, such as traditional post uploads, which are available immediately after signing and logging in. Fanbase is a great alternative for Clapper compatibility with Android and iOS-supported devices.

3. Plamfy

Plamfy is a live-streaming and social networking app like Clapper for Android devices. The platform allows creators to host and broadcast content through pre-recorded vlogs or live video streams.

The video chat app has a direct message feature enables its vast online community to keep in touch. Plamfy is available for Android devices on the Google Play Store.

4. Likee

Likee is a hot app like TikTok, with over 100 million users. The content-sharing and live-streaming app enables users to create and share short videos of themselves. The platform lets users live through personalised streams with innovative video effects.

Likee is a comprehensive video editor that empowers users to create high-quality content. It provides an exciting twist to its followers through augmented reality, magical AI, a vast collection of sticker effects, and a music library. The app is available on both Android and iOS devices.

5. Instagram

Instagram is an app that's better than Clapper. It is the oldest and most popular live-streaming, content-creation, and messaging application, boasting over 2 billion users. With a vast user base, Instagram's outreach is global.

For content creators with safe and effective content, the platform's remuneration policy of traditional posting is the right niche. Moreover, Instagram offers a pay-subscription model, Onlyfans, that offers monetisation for content creators.

The success of Onlyfans led to its wide adoption across other platforms. Furthermore, Instagram introduced 90-second short video reels, a powerful tool for content creators to unleash their creativity while generating revenue. 

6. Discord

Discord is a free instant video, text-audio messaging, and live-streaming platform quite popular among gamers. The application has private voice channels and chat rooms accessed via invite links.

Unlike other content creation platforms, this application is based on servers organised into topic-based channels. This user-friendly platform lets users hang out with friends, chat in groups, or host meetings.

Discord became popular owing to its inside jokes that used humour only understandable to members of a particular group or community of shared interest. Discord charges a $4.99 and $9.99 monthly subscription for users to use classier packages, including avatars.

7. Tango

Tango is an exhilarating social live-streaming application with over 400 million users. Tango has filters and effects to spice up uploads. With personalised feeds and live streaming, Tango provides the perfect medium for content creators to engage with fans and like-minded creators.

Through its vast global audience, Tango offers a lucrative alternative for content creators to market and earn money through live streaming. Tango is available for download on both Android and iOS devices.

8. Uplive

Uplive is unlike other messaging social media platforms mentioned above and primarily centres on live streaming. Being an open platform, Uplive can host various streaming options and boasts special app interfaces such as gift cards.

9. Thriller

Thriller is another Clapper alternative, specially designed for video makers. The platform lets users easily share high-quality videos by remixing music and using filter effects to upload social videos.

Thriller has a vibrant community that uploads entertainment content, starts new trend challenges, and connects audiences. The platform is available for download on both Android and iOS devices.

10. Vine

Vine is a content viewing and entertainment video streaming app like Clapper for iPhones. The application was created to allow content creators to forge better and stronger relationships with fans through having a Fan Mail tab.

Similarly to its cousin Huddle, Vine competes with Tik-Tok Clapper and can be used as a supplementary platform. The platform allows fans to support content creators through drop purchases, which they can send to their favourite artists.
