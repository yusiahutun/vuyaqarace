Lily-Bo-Peep: Lily Allen shows her cheeky side in a revealing blue outfit

Lily Allen clearly wasn't going for the shy and retiring look when she chose her latest stage outfit.

The Smile singer bared her bottom in an electric blue Little Bo Peep costume and stockings on the night it was revealed she had topped the charts with her new single The Fear.

She knocked Lady GaGa off the top spot, after entering the chart last week at 168 but this week stands proud at number one pushing Just Dance down to two.

Lily wore the £395 Agent Provocateur Marine Sailor body with their Europa Blue hold-up stockings, £18, and lacy Cara briefs £60.

She teamed the outfit with a pair of towering beige platform heels and was presented with a cake at the event to celebrate her comeback single The Fear hitting the number one spot.

Lily accessorised her cheeky blue look with bright pink lips and nails, which was contrasted by her dark long hair.

The cheeky number was in aid of her performance at G-A-Y but it didn't stop the 23-year-old wearing it out for a night of celebrating at nightspot Bungalow 8.

Lily, who has played at G-A-Y once before, performed classics from Alright, Still as well as tracks from her new album, It's Not Me, It's You.

And she lived up to her feisty reputation by returning to the stage for an encore dressed as Britney Spears to perform a cover of Womanizer.

Enlarge   Enlarge  

Barefaced cheek: Lily Allen wore the frilly playsuit post-show to party at club Bungalow 8

Her performance at G-A-Y comes in the wake of an interview with the Gay Times in which she said she once kissed identical lesbian twins.

The star denies she is gay but went into detail about the experience during the interview.

She told the magazine: 'That's the only time, but I have lesbian dreams a lot.'

This week Lily admitted she has turned to a therapist to get her through a crisis of confidence.

The singer says her moods are up and down and that she has arranged a session with a motivational expert to help her achieve a 'positive mental attitude'.

She has had her fair share of bad luck in the past, from her self-confessed troubled childhood to her tragic miscarriage last year.
