Taylor Swift Takes the Spotlight at Travis Kelce's NFL Game in a Custom Chiefs Jacket

Subzero temperatures have nothing on Taylor Swift—they simply mean she will be sporting cool new Kansas City Chiefs swag to keep herself warm.

In true form, Swift attended the freezing cold playoff game between the Chiefs and the Miami Dolphins at Missouri's Arrowhead Stadium yesterday in style. For the occasion, she bundled up in a bright red puffer coat emblazoned with her man Travis Kelce's name and jersey number, 87, all over it in white and yellow text. Also decorating the jacket were the words “Chiefs Kingdom,” of which Chiefs quarterback Patrick Mahomes recently told CBS Mornings’ Nate Burleson Swift is a part of. The “Now That We Don’t Talk” singer let her iconic outerwear shine, simply pairing it with a white knit beanie and black accents, including a turtleneck, skinny jeans, lace-up combat boots, and a purse. She rounded off the look with her signature red lip and black winged eyeliner and let her blonde hair fall in soft waves.

Swift’s one-of-a-kind creation was designed by Kristin Juszczyk, who is married to San Francisco 49ers fullback Kyle. Juszczyk took to Instagram to share a behind-the-scenes video of her work, also displaying the custom coat she made for Brittany Mahomes.

Jamie Squire

The pop superstar was all smiles sitting next to Kelce’s mom, Donna, and Brittany in the stands as they watched the Chiefs dominate the Dolphins 26-7. Swift also hyped up the crowd, high-fiving fellow Kansas City fans around her and later Swag Surfin—a celebratory Chiefs dance to F.L.Y.’s song by the same name.

Once the game concluded, Swift and Kelce made a fashionable celebratory exit, walking hand-in-hand. The tight end looked suave in black leather cargo pants, a black knit sweater, a fuzzy black-and-white checkered coat, black leather boots, and matching shades.
