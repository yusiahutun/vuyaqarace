The entire tunnel was evacuated and drivers warned to find alternative routes as traffic builds around the major London tunnel.

Transport for London said the southbound route has reopened but the northbound tunnel will not be open again any time soon.

Traffic came to a halt both sides of the tunnel, with long tailbacks of stand-still traffic reported.

Hundreds of people have been pictured stranded on the roads approaching the tunnel after it was evacuated.
