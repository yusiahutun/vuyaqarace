Ipurua is the Smallest Stadium in La Liga

Nestled in the charming town of Eibar, Spain, Ipurua Stadium holds the distinction of being the smallest venue in La Liga, the top tier of Spanish football. With a capacity of just over 7,000 spectators, this intimate setting provides an electrifying atmosphere for fans and players alike.

It Has a Unique Location

Situated in the heart of Eibar, Ipurua Stadium is surrounded by picturesque Basque mountains, creating a breathtaking backdrop for thrilling football matches. The stadium's close proximity to the town center adds to its distinctive charm, offering a truly immersive experience for visitors.

Ipurua’s Remarkable Rise to La Liga

Despite its modest size, Ipurua Stadium has been the stage for Eibar's remarkable journey to La Liga. The team's ascent from the lower divisions to the pinnacle of Spanish football is a testament to their unwavering spirit and the incredible support of their loyal fans.
