Engaged on Shahs of Sunset!

Shahs of Sunset Spoiler Alert:

Reza Farahan and Adam Neely are engaged!!!

Farahan confirmed the exciting news to People today, referring to his boyfriend of over three years as “the love of my life” and saying he was the one who popped the life-changing question.

Cameras, of course, captured the moment and it will air on next Tuesday’s season finale.

How did his family response to the big announcement?

 

“Not well!” Farahan tells the magazine. “Although they adore Adam, I didn’t tell them until last week so they didn’t appreciate the fact that I had kept such big news from them for so long. I waited five months! Persians are very opinionated and I didn’t want to hear anything negative around such a joyous event.”

Farahan may be accustomed to expensive, fancy occasions… but he says his wedding won’t be one of these.

“We’re just now starting to discuss the actual wedding,” he says. “It will definitely be a small and intimate affaire. Most likely somewhere exotic.”
