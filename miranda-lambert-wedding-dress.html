Get a Closer Look at Miranda Lambert's Gorgeous Wedding Dress

Fans all know by now that Miranda Lambert is (surprise!) a married woman. What they likely are now wondering about is: Where did she get that gorgeous wedding dress, and can we see a little more of it? While we can glimpse just enough of the singer's gown to tell it's a stunner, we'd sure like to see the whole thing.

Well, for those who would like to get a closer look at the details of Lambert's gown, it's readily available to see online, and even order for yourself should you be planning a wedding of your own! Popular wedding dress designer Lela Rose confirmed on Instagram that Lambert is wearing their "Canyon" style.

"What a beautiful bride," the designer enthuses.

The "Canyon" dress, which can be found on the Lela Rose website and seen on their model above, is entirely made of lace, with demurely long sleeves set off by a daring, plunging neckline. It's elegantly form-fitting, swirling into a bit of swoosh at the bottom, with a slight train (also composed of the same beautiful lace). Overall it is a classic design that will look lovely in photos for years to come, and is perfectly suited to a winter wedding.

Overall it is a quite different look than the one Lambert chose for her wedding to Blake Shelton in 2011. For that ceremony, she wore her mother's spaghetti-strap wedding gown, a pair of cowboy boots, and carried a "bouquet" created from jeweled brooches.

Lambert announced over Valentine's Day weekend that she'd secretly tied the knot with New York City police officer Brendan Mcloughlin, whom she termed the "love of my life." The exact date of the wedding has not been confirmed, nor are there many details available. A wedding venue in Henderson, Tenn. called Cogan's Farm shared the news on Facebook, adding that they like her style, so that may be at least one clue to where the happy event took place.

Lambert divorced Shelton in 2015, and has had an up-and-down love life since then. She dated singer Anderson East through early 2018, and reportedly began dating Turnpike Troubadours singer Evan Felker after. Her relationship with Mcloughlin has, remarkably, been kept private until now.
