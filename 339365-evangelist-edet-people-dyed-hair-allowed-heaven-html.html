Evangelist says people with dyed hair will not enter heaven

- An evangelist made claims about people allowed to enter heaven

- The man of God identified as Victor Edet stated that people with dyed hair will not have a chance

- Edet claimed that heaven is the house of God and people with gangster lifestyle will not be allowed in

An evangelist identified as Victor Edet has left many talking after saying people with dyed hair will not be allowed to enter the Kingdom of heaven.

The man of God noted that heaven is the home of almighty God and anyone who has a gangster lifestyle will not be allowed into heaven.

The man of God shared his opinion on his Facebook page where he openly condemned people with coloured hair.

Edet who hails from Nigeria posted different photos of famous people whose hair had been coloured in various ways asking how they expected to go to heaven.

"Heaven is the home of the Almighty God, therefore, anyone with a gangster lifestyle will not be allowed to enter therein. You cannot be painting your hair like this and be thinking of getting to heaven," read the post.
