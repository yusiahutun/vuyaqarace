Sienna Miller looked effortlessly chic in a cream outfit as she departed a nail salon in Manhattan on Tuesday.

 The Anatomy of a Scandal star styled her long blonde tresses loose from a centre parting and accessorised with a pair of gold hoop earrings. 

It comes after Sienna recently made a number of sweet appearance with boyfriend Oli, who is 15 years her junior.
