Her curvy figure has made her an inspiration for women across the world, but Kate Upton admits she wants to reduce her generous breast size so she can squeeze into raunchier swimwear.

The 21-year-old model, who boasts a 34D chest, shot to fame after gracing the cover of iconic swimwear issue of US magazine Sports Illustrated with only a coat of paint protecting her modesty.

But while her stunning size 10 figure has since graced towering New York billboards, featured on the catwalk for Victoria’s Secret and been splashed across the cover of fashion bible Vogue, Kate says she’s keen on breast reduction.

‘I wish I had smaller boobs every day of my life as I would love to wear spaghetti tops braless or go for the smallest bikini design,’ she told Natalie Edwards of The Sun On Sunday, adding: ‘I know I say I wish I had smaller boobs – and that’s true because at least twice a day I wish that. But the grass is always greener, as they say!’

While she’s won praise for promoting a healthier body image than the more traditional, waif-life figures paraded across catwalks and fashion shoots, Kate has been harshly criticized in some quarters. 

‘I love my boobs and I’m proud of my size,’ she said. ‘That’s an important message to young girls – love who you are and be proud of who you are. 

Anti-fashion: Kate's size-10 frame - unconventional by catwalk standards - has won praise, but has also been harshly labelled 'fat' by critics

‘It’s so easy to feel negative about yourself and it is totally self-destructive. If you can only love and accept who you are you will be a lot happier.’

Kate found fame after responding to casting call from Elite Model Management in 2008, a move that led to her first major contract as the face of Guess Jeans.

Small film roles followed in Ben Stiller comedy Tower Heist and The Three Stooges – in which she stars alongside Effie Murphy – before she landed a starring role in new comedy The Woman alongside Cameron Diaz.

Starring role: The American model is currently featuring in new film The Other Woman alongside Cameron Diaz (L) and Leslie Mann (C)

In the film, which focuses on the plight of three women who seek revenge after discovering they have been cheated on by the dame man, Diaz refers to Kate’s body as: ‘… the perfect 10, lemon tart, double D… obviously.’

But the model , currently dating baseball star Justin Verlander, is equally complimentary of her size 8 co-star, adding: ‘In my eyes, Cameron has THE perfect figure.’

Elsewhere Kate has been given further affirmation of her role model status by being named Model Of The Year at the 10th Style Awards during New York Fashion Week. 

The Other Women is released across the UK on April 23.
