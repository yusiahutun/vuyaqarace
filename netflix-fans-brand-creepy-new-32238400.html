Netflix fans brand creepy new doc 'nightmare fuel' as voices 'freak them out'

Netflix viewers have been left freaked out by new documentary, Can I Tell You A Secret, as they labelled it "nightmare fuel".

The two-part true crime docu-series landed on the streaming platform last week (February 21) and has become the talk of the town on social media. The programme follows the story of one of the UK's most prolific cyberstalkers and the victims who he taunted over an 11-year period.

The culprit behind the heinous crimes was Matthew Hardy, who taunted at least 62 different women across the UK. In the documentary, four of Matthew's victims come forward to disclose how he ruined their lives with his psychological warfare.

The harrowing Netflix show, based on Sirin Kale's investigation for The Guardian, delved into the victim's long journey to justice as well as the crimes that Hardy committed. Hardy would attempt to befriend his victims by posing as someone they knew or a stranger who pretended to know something about them.

Hardy would often spread lies about his victims to their friends and family, he would create fake accounts in the victims' names, stalk their Facebook accounts and share pictures and videos to other social media users pretending to be them. Hardy went as far as causing rifts in relationships as he would pose as the victim and send sexually explicit messages to the victims' friends and family members.

The crazy cyber behaviour didn't just stop online. Hardy caused some his victims' anxieties to hit the roof as he also physically stalked them too.

In the Netflix documentary which illustrated Hardy's crimes, a creepy voice was used when the messages that Hardy sent was read out. The dark programme led many viewers to speak out online as they shared how petrified the true crime series made them feel.

Heading over to social media platform X, one fan said: "Watching Can I Tell You A Secret? on Netflix and the voices they use to read the messages are freaking me out." Another penned: "Can I Tell You A Secret? on Netflix is perfect nightmare fuel, especially if you’re a woman, and what did I, a woman, do? I watched it right before bed."

While a third shared: "Can I Tell You A Secret? on Netflix I can say is one of the craziest and scariest documentary I’ve watched. It’s literally about stalkers and their victims, I recommend y’all to watch especially women."

Hardy was finally convicted for his crimes in 2022. He was sentenced to nine years in prison after he pleaded guilty to three counts of stalking with intent to cause alarm or distress, two counts of stalking without intent to cause alarm, and also breaching a restraining order that was made against him from 2013.
