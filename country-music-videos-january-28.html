Will Brantley Gilbert Top the Week's Most Popular Country Videos?

Brantley Gilbert has released a cool new video for his new song, "How to Talk to Girls." Will he top the most popular country music videos for the week?

The formidable-looking country singer with the heart of gold is up against quite a bit of competition as Nashville's music industry opens back up for business after its annual post-holiday break. Lee Brice, Tracy Lawrence, Laci Kay Booth, Morgan Wade, Josh Mirenda and Tenille Arts all have great new videos out this week that are looking for support in the countdown of the week's top country music videos.

The Top 10 looks different once again this week as old favorites have aged out of voting and new clips have come up for consideration. Home Free are back at No. 1, while Jim Stanard's latest holds down the No. 2 slot. Jess Kellie Adams and Lauren Mascitti round out the Top 5, and fans can say farewell to Mascitti's "One Life Stand" video, which has had an enormously successful run in the countdown but has aged out of consideration. Danielle Bradbery, George Strait, Kane Brown, Josh Turner and Miranda Lambert complete the Top 10 this time around.

Which artists do you want to see at the top of the countdown next time around? Voting has been unusually competitive in recent months, so if you want to see your favorites come out on top, you've got to vote, and keep right on voting. Be sure to check back next Friday to see who rules the countdown this week.

Due to an update, the voting poll looks different than it used to. When you vote now, you need to scroll up afterward to see the captcha. Once you verify yourself, your vote will be counted as usual. 

Note: Fans can vote for one video, once every hour until the poll closes next Friday at 12PM ET. The weekly Top 10 will be revealed immediately after the poll closes. Videos are retired after six months, when fans lose interest or when an artist releases a new video for a current single.
