Sandman is going on a tour! Adam Sandler tickets are selling out quickly for his latest tour aptly called the I Missed You Tour.

The Uncut Gems star will embark in a three-month comedy tour produced by Live Nation on October 12 at Rogers Arena in Vancouver, BC, and make stops in Palm Desert, Memphis, Toronto, and more before closing in Denver at Ball Arena on December 12, 2023. 

“When you’re just doing stand-up and you got to go from joke to joke to joke and you forget the order or you forget what the heck the punchline is or what the subject was, oh, man, you’re – that is a rough one,” the You’re So Not Invited to My Bat Mitzvah producer and star recalled on NPR’s All Things Considered. “Especially, I was so young, and I’d stare at people going, we paid for this idiot and he’s forgetting his lines? So the guitar helped relax me.”
