Early Life

James Marsden was born on September 18, 1973. His birthplace is in Stillwater, Oklahoma, U.S.A. Marsden is currently 50 years old. This personality’s full name is James Paul Marsden. His horoscope is Virgo.

Further, his father is James Luther Marsden who is a food safety advisor to LexiGene Industries, and his mother is Kathleen who is a nutritionist. Hence, his parents divorced when he was nine years old.

Moreover, he has four siblings, two younger sisters, and two brothers.

Education

Marsden attended Hefner Middle School and Putnam City North High School. Later, he attended Oklahoma State University, where he studied broadcast journalism. Also, he was a member of the Delta Tau Delta fraternity.

Career

James Marsden is a popular American actor, singer, and former model who is famous for his work in The Notebook, 10th & Wolf, The Alibi, Touched by an Angel, and many more. This personality went on to star in various genre films, including Hairspray, which was a critical and commercial success and won many awards. Also, he sang two songs for the film’s soundtrack which has been certified Platinum by the RIAA.

Moreover, he also appeared in the films Sex Drive, The Box, and Straw Dogs. Further, Marsden has also had starring roles in family-friendly films such as Enchanted, Hop, and Disenchanted, and leading roles in 27 Dresses, The Best of Me, Tom Wachowski in Sonic the Hedgehog, and Sonic the Hedgehog 2.

Furthermore, he starred in a range of independent films such as Small Apartments, Bachelorette, and Robot & Frank. Later, he returned to television guest-starring in Modern Family and playing Liz Lemon’s love interest, Criss Chros in 30 Rock in a supporting role.

Additionally, he played Stu Redman in the Paramount+ miniseries The Stand. Further, he has a role in Netflix’s upcoming film Unfrosted: The Pop Tart Story in 2020.
