Emmerdale's longest-serving cast member starred in Swedish sex films before ITV soap fame

Emmerdale icon Chris Chittell has quite a chequered past away from the ITV soap.

Few can remember the serial drama before Eric Pollard joined in 1986 as a wheeler dealer turned bed-and-breakfast king, who's romanced a string of ladies, including favourites Val Pollard (Charlie Hardwick) and Faith Dingle (Sally Dexter).

However, before he got his big break on one of telly's most beloved soaps, Chris tried a different route to get into acting – starring in a series of Swedish porn films. His list of credits include the bluntly titled Let Us Play Sex, Sex in Sweden, and Practise Makes Perfect, which is also known as Girl On Her Knees.

"One was an antiques business. Another was a Jewish-Italian restaurant in Hatton Garden, London. "I took them to pay off some heavy-duty people and clear the bills." But Chris' daring venture into soft porn lead the actor fearing he may never work in the UK.

"I always thought I was going to be fired, even after years in Emmerdale," he explained. I thought I would never work in England again because of the content of those films. Thank God I've been on the up ever since."

Chris has found himself sinking his teeth into a number of meaty storylines in the soap. In upcoming scenes, Eric is close to being pushed over the edge and into a downward spiral as he faces his first symptoms of Parkinson's. He revealed to Mandy Dingle (Lisa Riley) that he was terrified as he'd been diagnosed with the disease earlier this year.
