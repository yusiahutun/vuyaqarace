Career

Nicky began his career at the age of 14 years by releasing the album, Distinto A Los Demas. The album did not become a hit immediately. However, it gained some recognition in the music industry.

After the release of the album, he combined efforts with Daddy Yankee to form a music group called Los Canaris. Later on, the duo split and each embarked on a solo career. Their friendship was strained because of Nicky's addiction to prescription pills.

Daddy distanced himself from Jam after being arrested in 2002. This was after the addiction proved too much for him to handle. Towards the end of 2002, he released his debut reggaeton album, Vida Escante.

It was a notorious hit in the urban genre and had songs like I'm Dying, I'm Not Your Husband, Chambonea, and I'm Leaving the Party. This album reached #4 on Top Tropical Albums, #23 on Top Latin Albums, charted on Billboard, and got an important position on Latin Tropical Airplay.
