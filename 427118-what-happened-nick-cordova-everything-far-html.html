Who was Nick Cordova?

On October 16, 1979, Nick Cordova was born in Scottsdale, Arizona, the United States of America. He was raised by his mother, Theresa.

Nick graduated from Chandler High School in 1998. During his high school years, he was heavily involved in sports, especially football and wrestling.

After finishing his studies, Nick started working as a personal trainer. He stuck to this role for a couple of years before diverting his interests into heating, ventilation, and air conditioning. He later bought Gilbert Air and worked hard to provide for his family.

Nick Cordova was a hardworking man, and many considered him a jack of all trades. Aside from his business interests, he was a family man who spent most of his free time with his wife, kids, and friends. He used to hang out with family either outside or working on one of his automobiles.

Nick Cordova from Arizona was found dead at about 5:30 pm on May 27, 2020, inside Gilbert Air, Heating and Cooling at 511 W. Guadalupe Road. However, the police did not release the information to the public until recently, when they required help identifying the primary suspects.

Two possible suspects

Even though detectives have not stated how Nick Cordova died, they have identified two suspects fleeing the scene shortly after his murder. And one of the suspects was captured by surveillance video at a nearby convenience store.

The two suspects in the murder case arrived at the gas station near Nick Cordova’s business minutes after the murder. Unfortunately, no specifics about the two individuals have been released by the police.

The Gilbert Police urges the public to watch the video and report any information they might have about the two people. The person appears to be 5'8'' with a heavy build. According to police reports, he seems to be either Hispanic or Native American.
