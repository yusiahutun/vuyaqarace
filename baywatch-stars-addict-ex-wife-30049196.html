Baywatch star's addict ex-wife rummaging for food again after 'acrimonious' breakup

Troubled former model Loni Willison has again been spotted rummaging for food in the dumpsters of Los Angeles, nine years on from splitting with her Baywatch star ex, Jeremy Jackson.

The pair were married for two years before going their separate ways in acrimonious circumstances after the 39-year old accused Jackson of of trying to strangle her in the midst of a drunken fracas.

Willison, who was also an actress and best known for her role as Kira Mickaels in the thriller TV series, Expose, rapidly spiralled into life as a homeless drug addict in the aftermath of their breakup.

Now, in new snaps, she can be seen desperately looking for something to eat with her trolley full of clothes beside her.

In an interview with X17 Online earlier this month, Willison blamed her woes on Jackson.

Asked where her life went wrong, she responded: "My ex-husband. Getting married. At least I got divorced. He set everything up to do this to me."

She dropped off the grid entirely in 2018, and was never again seen with her long blonde hair and buxom appearance.

Instead, her bronzed body was replaced with dirty clothes and a make-up free face, along with bruised fingers.

Loni told the Daily Mail that year that she hadn’t showered for 12 months, and kept herself as filthy as possible to avoid being assaulted or raped.

Despite this, she appeared to be relatively content, despite her tragic circumstances, as she told The Sun: "I haven't spoken to Jeremy. I don't want to speak to my friends, I'm doing just fine. I don't want anyone to help me."

She continued: "I haven't got a cell phone. I've got food and I've got a place to sleep. I get money here and there and there's food in the bins and near the stores. There's lots here."

After years sleeping rough, meanwhile, she recently confessed she is in a "lot of pain" and "can’t live inside anywhere" due to a sensitivity to electricity.

She claims someone "electrocuted her every day", which means she’s more sensitive to electrical currents.

She told X17: "I think because [I pick] up on the electricity, I also pick up on other things, like certain chemicals or batteries, or fuses, like wire, different types of metals. So I think my body even filters that kind of stuff."

She added: "I’d have to go through a big X-ray, sonogram machine or something to see that. It’s pretty intense."

Following the pair's divorce, Jackson remained in the limelight and featured on Celebrity Big Brother in 2015.

However, he was thrown out of the TV compound for pulling open fellow contestant Chloe Goodman's dressing gown.

The Ex On The Beach star said at the time: "He went to my dressing gown top and put my boob out."
