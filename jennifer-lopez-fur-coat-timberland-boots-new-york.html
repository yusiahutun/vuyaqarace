Jennifer Lopez Pairs Timbs With the Fur Coat She Can't Stop Wearing

Jennifer Lopez may be a lot of things—a performer, an actor, a Birkin connoisseur, a bling cup ambassador—but she’s also still just Jenny from the block.

On Sunday, the multi-hyphenate paid homage to her sartorial roots on a casual stroll in New York City, where she zhuzhed up her laid-back white T-shirt and baggy pleated jeans with wardrobe staples characteristic of her early-’00s style. These included a classic pair of Timberland boots (the quintessential NYC shoe), large gold hoop earrings, and—the pièce de résistance—a huge fluffy cream fur jacket with warm caramel undertones.

The Image Direct

Lopez has worn this particular jacket on repeat for weeks now. She memorably styled the outerwear during Couture Week in Paris, where she wore it with a white top and ivory cargo pants. For a nighttime outing in New York last week, she also pulled out the coat, that time to layer it over a screen-printed T-shirt and white puddle pants from Les Tien. She tucked the hem of her pants into chunky beige and white snow boots with Velcro straps, then accessorized with a white baseball cap, a small studded crossbody purse in a minty pistachio hue, and a large Gucci duffel bearing the house’s signature monogram as well as her own embroidered initials.

Lopez’s affinity for fur coats is well documented. She had another major fur moment last weekend, following her musical performances on Saturday Night Live. After the show, she was photographed exiting the venue in an icy-white fur coat layered over a crystal-embellished David Koma dress and sparkly silver Christian Louboutin boots.
