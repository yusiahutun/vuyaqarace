Elon Musk warned to delete Amber Heard photo where she dressed up as his crush

Elon Musk has shared an unseen picture of Amber Heard and fans want him to delete it!

Elon Musk and Amber Heard dated officially in 2017. While the relationship between X (formerly Twitter) owner and Driver Angry actress did not last long, both celebrities talked about maintaining their friendship. Years later, Amber Heard and Elon Musk opened up about their dating life in a new biography.

Elon Musk shares Amber Heard’s photo dressed as Mercy from Overwatch

Elon Musk shared a rare picture of Amber Heard cosplaying as Overwatch’s Mercy after his latest biography written by Walter Isaacson shared a sneak peek of how the actress commissioned the special costume for Elon.

The biography also gives a glimpse of the tumultuous relationship between the two where the SpaceX CEO called it ‘brutal’ and his brother Kimbal Musk went on to call it, “toxic” and “a nightmare.”

Fans urge Elon, “you should delete this”

Elon Musk’s sudden sharing of a private photo has shocked fans as they urged Tech Mogul to delete the picture.

A concerned fan posted, “Hopefully this was approved by her.. we don’t need another controversy.”

“You should delete this,” another user advised Elon while one quipped, “In which folder are you keeping this photo?”

“Elon loves fire, and sometimes it burns him”

Elon Musk did not hold back talking about his relationship with Amber Heard in the new biography where he called himself a “fool for love.” On the other hand, the TV and movie actress seemed much more positive about the former relationship as she gushed, “I love him very much. Elon loves fire, and sometimes it burns him.”

Speaking of Elon Musk’s relationships, his brother revealed a more intimate side of Elon sharing, that he sometimes “falls in love with these people who are really mean to him.”

“They’re beautiful, no question, but they have a very dark side and Elon knows that they’re toxic,” he added.
