Rhonda Ross Kendrick Happily Married To Husband Rodney Kendrick

Rhonda Ross Kendrick is a well-known American actress, singer, and songwriter. After few years of dating, Rhonda, and her boyfriend, Rodney Kendrick a jazz musician and pianist, walk down the aisle on 14 September 1997. From that happiest day of their life, there is no looking back.

Rhonda Ross Kendrick Family Background

Rhonda Suzanne Silberstein was born on 14 August 1971 in Los Angeles, California to parents Diana Ross, a famous singer, and Robert Ellis Silberstein, an American music executive, and businessman.

Her mother Diana was pregnant with Rhonda when she married Robert Ellis Silberstein. Though Robert is not Rhonda's biological father, she is very close to him.

Berry Gordy, the founder of the Motown record label is the biological father to Rhonda. Rhonda came to know about her biological father when she was 13. She soon came to know about the reason behind physical differences between her and her half-sisters. 

 

Rhonda Ross Kendrick with her siblings

Rhonda is the eldest of her five siblings. Her siblings are Tracee Ellis Ross an actress, Chudney Ross, Evan Ross, Ross Arne Naess, and Rockwell. Since Rhonda was born and raised in an entertaining environment, she was extremely interested in acting from the beginning.

She is best known for her appearance in Another World, for which she was nominated for Daytime Emmy Award 1998. She released her first studio album In Case You Didn't Know in July 2016 with singles Summer Day and In Case You Didn't Know.
