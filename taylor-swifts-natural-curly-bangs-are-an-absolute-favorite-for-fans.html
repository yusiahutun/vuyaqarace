Taylor Swift's natural curly bangs are an 'absolute favorite' for fans

Taylor Swift’s legions of fans have decided on their favorite hairstyle for the star, and it predates much of her modern career.

Taylor Swift, like all celebrities, has gone through various style changes over the years. While she is now partial to a sparkly leotard on stage, that wasn’t always the case. The same could be said of the celebrity‘s hair, which has gone through numerous subtle updates while keeping its signature blonde shade. 

Taylor Swift needs to bring the bangs back

Though Taylor has gone through many notable hairstyles over the decades – we’re looking at you red wig in the Bad Blood video – some remain firmly number one in the brains of her fans. 

In a recent viral Twitter post that garnered over 61k views, a fan account shared a collage of her most beloved T-Swift hairstyle alongside the caption: “Taylor Swift with curly bangs are my absolute fav.”

The collage of 16 photographs showed the celebrity at various ages with her bangs wispy, bouncy, and outright curly.

She looked incredibly baby-faced in a number of them, suggesting the throwback look is something she hasn’t embraced fully in several years. 

Though it’s unclear whether Taylor will donn her throwback hairstyle in the years to come, there is deafening hunger from fans who called for the curly bangs.

One person wrote: “SHE LOOKS SO BEAUTIFUL.”

“My all time favorite Taylor hairstyle,” another quipped, as someone else added, “Yessssssssssssssssssss curls are giving me life . They’ll be back slowly but surly heheheheheh.”

It’s not the first time

Similar joy over Taylor’s hair was shared last week during the Australian leg of her Eras Tour. She fully embraced her natural waves as she performed for the Aussie crowd, sending her fans into an absolute meltdown in the process. 

“TAYLOR ALISON SWIFT PLS NEVER PICK UP A HAIR STRAIGHTENER AGAIN,” one fan screamed on social media.

“Unpopular opinion: Taylor Swift’s naturally wavy curly hair is way more beautiful than the straight hair she has had for years now!!!!!!” another person argued.
