Bachelor in Paradise 2018 spoilers: Venmo John net worth, plus who does he end up with?

John Graham, introduced to the world on The Bachelorette, is currently looking for love on Bachelor in Paradise.

So far he’s made connections with Caroline Lunny and Jubilee Sharpe. The app developer is now caught in the middle of a potentially-ugly love triangle, but Bachelor in Paradise fans are hopeful he’ll come out of it with his heart unscathed.

Bachelor Nation spoiler blogger Reality Steve has reported that Venmo John, as he’s affectionately been called by fans, does find love on the reality show. His partner in Paradise won’t be who viewers are expecting, though.

John Graham, also known as Venmo John, is currently appearing on Bachelor in Paradise season five

Venmo John connected with The Bachelor's Jubilee Sharpe on Bachelor in Paradise, but their love may not last the season

Want to know more about Venmo John, like his net worth and who he ends up with on Bachelor in Paradise? Check it out.

Who is Venmo John?

John Graham, also known as Venmo John, is a 29-year-old San Francisco, California native. He works as a software engineer.

His portfolio includes credits for developing Venmo. According to a Medium post written by John, he was the fifth employee to start working at Venmo back in 2011. He worked there for about three years.

After leaving the company, he transitioned to Fin., which is essentially an artificially intelligent butler.

Venmo John also has a cooking show called Kitchen Talk. On it, he and a friend serve up different recipes and ideas for food-focused gatherings.

John appears to be close with his mother, whom he happens to share an August 1 birthday with according to Instagram. He also has a sister called Laura whom he is close with.

His profile on the photo-sharing site suggests that he’s also actively involved in volunteering with the elderly, teaching older men and women how to use and keep up with technology. He regularly posts photos with his partner from the program.

John is an outdoorsman and seems to enjoy the beach and other adventurous settings. He leads a very active lifestyle, frequently working out.

Venmo John net worth

An exact figure for John Graham’s net worth hasn’t been pinned down, but he’s surely one of the more successful people to pass through the Bachelor franchise. According to United States Bureau of Labor Statistics figures, the average salary of a software developer in 2017 was about $104,000 (£81,671).

Due to the fact that John has worked with some high-level companies, he probably made even more than that estimation.

Braintree bought Venmo in 2012 for $26.2 million (£20.5m) and PayPal bought Braintree the following year for $800 million (£627.9m). John may have received millions in the sale of Venmo because he was an original employee.

John now works for another successful tech company called Fin., which is a digital assistant service that uses artificial intelligence to meet user’s needs.

What is Venmo app?

Venmo is a popular money sharing app. It allows users to send and request money from friends without having to go directly through their bank. The app can also be used to pay certain retailers when shopping from your mobile phone.

Who developed Venmo?

John Graham and a team of developers worked on Venmo. John did not found the app, nor is he believed to be the soul developer.

He was among the first employees to join Venmo after its founding and he worked there for three years before leaving.

Who is Jubilee on Bachelor in Paradise?

One of the first women to connect with John Graham on Bachelor in Paradise was Jubilee Sharpe. She’s an ex-military woman from Florida who was first introduced to Bachelor fans during Ben Higgins’s season of the show.

Jubilee went far on The Bachelor, but didn’t win Ben’s heart. She did, however, steal the hearts of viewers everywhere when she shared the heart-breaking story of her life back in Haiti.

Jubilee’s entire family, with the exception of her sister, died there before she was adopted.

Since appearing on The Bachelor, Jubilee has appeared on Bachelor in Paradise, but hasn’t had much luck finding love. Now that she’s back on the show she’s got high hopes for her future.

Who does Venmo John end up with on Bachelor in Paradise?

According to Reality Steve, John Graham doesn’t find love with Caroline Lunny or Jubilee Sharpe on Bachelor in Paradise. Instead, he connects with Olivia Goethals.

He’s one of few people who will leave the show with a partner. John and Olivia won’t get engaged at the end of the season, however.

There will be a proposal this season — two, in fact. Viewers will get to see the moment Jared Haibon proposed to Ashley Iaconetti while visiting the set of Bachelor in Paradise. They’ll also see Jordan Kimball propose to Jenna Cooper.

Speaking on his happy ending in Paradise, Jordan told Refinery29: ‘I do find someone for me in Paradise.

‘It ends on a very good note.’
