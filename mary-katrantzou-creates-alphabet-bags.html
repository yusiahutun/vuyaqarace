Mary Katrantzou Creates Alphabet Bags

Consider the search for your Summer beach bag over. British designer Mary Katrantzou has partnered with Matches on a 26-piece limited-edition collection of alphabet-printed handbags. The collection, which launches on April 22nd, was inspired by Katrantzou's typography influenced Resort 2015 collection, and each bag is printed with a stylized letter and collage of architectural, graphic and statement prints. Only 20 of each bag will be made, so we suggest you run (don't walk!), for your special letter.

Bags retail for $1,070 and will available on matchesfashion.com on April 22nd.
