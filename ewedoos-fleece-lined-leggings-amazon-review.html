My Honest Review of the Ewedoos Fleece-Lined Leggings From Amazon

I haven't had the best relationship with "real" pants since the pandemic hit. Not that I really loved them even before that, but thanks to discovering the plethora of comfy sweatpants during quarantine (and gaining some weight), pulling on jeans nowadays literally makes me go, "Ugh."

I knew I had to find a solution for this during the winter — because my usual layering of jeans over fleece tights was not happening — so I took a chance on a pair of fleece-lined leggings from Amazon, hoping they'd keep me warm in freezing temperatures and also be super comfortable. And reader, they turned out to be exactly what I was looking for.

Shop now: $30 (Originally $40); amazon.com

The Ewedoos Fleece-Lined Leggings have forever changed my winter wardrobe for the better. Other than the (rare) times I have a night out and put on actual pants, I've exclusively been wearing these leggings for the past two winters. They have a high rise waistband that isn't constricting, two roomy pockets that hold my iPhone securely, and are moisture-wicking. The fleece lining keeps me warm when it's as low as 20 degrees Fahrenheit out, and I don't feel like I'm overheating when it's 30 to 40 degrees, either.

The first time I purchased them I got them in a size medium — they did feel like they ran a little small, but they stretched out a bit over time. This winter I decided to size up and I'm glad I did; they fit and look even better. So if you're in-between sizes, I do suggest sizing up. The Ewedoos leggings go up to a size 2XL and are available in seven colors, including black, heather gray, dark green, and purple. The black pair is currently on sale for $30, while other colors are discounted down to $33.

Shop now: $33 (Originally $40); amazon.com

If I were you, I'd snag a couple of pairs of the Ewedoos Fleece-Lined Leggings while they're on sale — trust me, you'll never want to put on a pair of pants again after trying them.
