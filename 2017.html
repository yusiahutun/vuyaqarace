Daughtry To Hit The Road In 2018

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Rocker Chris Daughtry and his band Daughtry announced plans for a tour of North America, set to hit the road in Spring, 2018.

The tour launches on March 3rd at the Capitol Theater in Clearwater, Florida, and runs through April 20th, when the band is scheduled to perform at Buffalo Run Casino in Miami, Oklahoma.

Other stops on the tour include the Van Wezel in Sarasota on March 8th; The Hippodrome in Baltimore on March 18th; and the Mohegan Sun Arena on Apil 6th.

While Daughtry hasnt’ released a new studio album since 2013’s “Baptized” the band has launched a new online gaming website called “Daughtry Games” that allows you to play casino-style games such as blackjack and slots. Instead of winning real gelt with the games, you instead can earn Daughtry Dollars and ‘XP’ which the site says will let you earn rewards from the band such as merch, tickets or ‘Daughtry Experiences.’ If you’re unlucky at casino-style games, you can also spend real money for Daughtry Dollars to increase your chances of winning, however, why you’d want to do that is not entirely clear.
