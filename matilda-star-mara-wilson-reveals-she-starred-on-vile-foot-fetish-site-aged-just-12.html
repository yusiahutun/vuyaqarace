Matilda star Mara Wilson reveals she starred on vile foot fetish site aged just 12

MARA Wilson, who rose to fame as a child in films such as Matilda and Mrs Doubtfire, has revealed that she stumbled across pornography websites which promised nude images of her, and pictures of her feet when she was just 12-years-old.

The actress who is now 29, also shared that she discovered she was going through puberty when a casting director suggested she wear training bras.

And around the same time - before she had even entered her teens, she read a website that remarked that she was entering “the awkward years, when she’ll be old enough to have breasts, but not old enough to show them legally.”

Writing in her autobiography Where Am I Now?: True Stories Of Girlhood And Accidental Fame, Mara adds: "The next page of search results linked to a website with a description that said, 'If you want Mara Wilson nude and sex pictures, click here.'”

"A few months later I found out I was listed on a foot fetish site that catalogued scenes in movies where children’s feet could be seen. Then there was the fan letter from an adult man who said he loved my legs and wanted my lip print on an index card."

Mara has now turned her back on acting, instead pursuing a career as a playwright, and is involved in the storytelling and comedy scene in New York.

After the 2016 Orlando shootings in which gay men were targeted at a nightclub, Mara came out as bisexual.
