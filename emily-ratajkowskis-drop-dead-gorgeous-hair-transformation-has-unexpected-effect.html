Emily Ratajkowski's 'drop-dead gorgeous' hair transformation has unexpected effect

Emily Ratajkowski recently debuted her red fiery hair transformation on Instagram, a look no one was expecting. The 32-year-old model swapped her signature brown hair for a fiery red look, ready for the summer ahead.

Ever since Emily Ratajkowski appearance in Maroon 5’s “Love Somebody” music video in 2012, Em has been a style icon for many, and 11 years later, she’s still as influential as ever. Brb, going to get some red hair dye.

Emily Ratajkowski shows off her new red hair
