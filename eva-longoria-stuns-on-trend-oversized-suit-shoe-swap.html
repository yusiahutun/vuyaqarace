Eva Longoria debuts new look in suit - but did you notice her shoes?

The Desperate Housewives star showed workwear can be cool in this fun shoe swap look

    We love an oversized suit moment. But even more so, we love Eva Longoria having an oversized suit moment. The Desperate Housewives star, 48, stunned when she attended the 2023 SCAD Savannah Film Festival in the southern state of Georgia to talk about her long and illustrious career which was followed by a screening of her newest endeavour as the director of a biographical film Flamin' Hot.

    The actress, who has recently released , an eating tour docuseries through the South American country, arrived wearing a super oversized grey single-breasted blazer in a boxy fit. 

    She paired the blazer with a trendy buttoned-up waistcoat and matching oversized grey trousers but it was her footwear that caught our eye. 

    When pictured alongside the likes of Lulu Wang and Kelly Fremon Craig and walking onto the stage the star wore a pair of pointed perspex heels embellished with large diamontes, super glamorous. 

    And yet when the star was pictured on stage she was seen having made a shoe swap, styling her oversized suit look with a pair of black patent stilettos for a more classic look.

    Beyond the unexplained shoe swap, it was also Eva's accessories getting our attention. The mother-of-one mixed her metals opting for a pair of silver hoops and a gold medallion necklace.

    Eva opted for her signature neutral makeup look with full and flirty lashes, a peachy cheek, and a nude satin lip.

    Keeping in with the sleek masc appeal of the three-piece suit, Eva wore her hair slicked back in a bun to reveal her incredible bone structure. Completing the look was an understated stiletto nail look in a nude shade.

    The actress is on our style radar for a reason. We were stunned by the vibrant yellow look she wore to the TIAH 5th Anniversary Soiree in Los Angeles alongside Jeff Bezos' fiancée Lauren Sanchez. She styled the dramatic one-shoulder strapless gown with a pair of reflective gold pointed-toe shoes and wore her hair in a chic updo.

    She has also been known to favour a cool casual style. She rocked a must-see ab-baring white blouse with a pair of midwash skinny jeans alongside Jason Momoa at a special fundraiser to benefit those affected by the fires in Maui. 

    Eva also made an appearance in a dress which distracted from the rare appearance of her billionaire husband at the event. She wore a bodycon halterneck nude dress with cut-out details with matching nude accessories when attending the red carpet to celebrate the 15th anniversary of production company Bambú in Madrid alongside her film executive husband José Bastón.

    Eva is the stepmother to José's three adult children – Natalia, 27, and Mariana and Jose, both 19. During a People magazine interview prior to their nuptials, Eva said that José's three children were "such a gift" in her life.

    Eva has since given birth to a son, five-year-old Santiago with her husband, whom she married in 2016.
