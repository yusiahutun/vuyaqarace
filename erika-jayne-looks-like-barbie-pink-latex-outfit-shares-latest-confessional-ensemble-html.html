The Real Housewives star also explained the importance and special meaning behind the title of her residency: Bet It All On Blonde. 

'Look, I think it’s no secret what I’ve been going through these last couple years,' she admitted, referencing towards her recent money woes and her estranged husband, Tom Girardi's, embezzlement scandal. 

'And you do, in life, have to bet on yourself. Especially in this business. You have to be willing to take risks, and Vegas is a betting town, so I bet it all on myself. I rolled the dice and I’m cashing in,' she told Billboard. 

Girardi's alleged theft and fraud came to light back in 2020, the same year that Jayne filed for divorce from the disgraced lawyer. The divorce has yet to be officially finalized. 

Earlier this year in February, he was indicted for allegedly stealing $18 million from clients as well as families of the victims of the tragic 2018 Lion Air plane crash. He has also been facing civil litigation over claims that he additionally stole money from his own law firm. 

Later in February, following his indictment, Tom pled not guilty to the charges, and was later released on a $250,000 bond. 

Last month in March, it has also come to light that Girardi, who was diagnosed with Alzheimer's in 2021, gave more than $1million in gifts and payments to a State Bar of California investigator and his wife, according to newly-released reports.

The reports detail how Girardi kept an 'extensive network of connections at all levels' of the State Bar of California which is tasked with regulating the legal profession - with dozens of client complaints against Girardi closed without any action taken.
