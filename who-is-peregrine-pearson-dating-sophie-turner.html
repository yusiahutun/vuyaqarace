Who Is Peregrine Pearson, Sophie Turner's Rumored New Beau?

Does Sophie Turner have a new man in her life? It may appear so, according to a PDA-filled photograph that The Sun published last October.

In the picture, the Game of Thrones alum can be seen kissing British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson during a recent trip to Paris. Later that same day, both Pearson and Turner were at the Stade de France, where Turner helped co-present the Rugby World Cup trophy, according to E! News.

The rumored romance came just a few months after the actor and husband Joe Jonas announced their decision to divorce. “After four wonderful years of marriage we have mutually decided to amicably end our marriage,” they said in a joint statement shared to social media. “There are many speculative narratives as to why but, truly this is a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy for us and our children.”

Ahead, we break down everything you need to know about Turner’s rumored new beau.

Dave Benett//Getty Images

Peregrine Pearson (left) at a 2021 party for Gentleman’s Journal.

He is the heir to the Cowdray estate.

Pearson is the eldest son of Michael Orlando Weetman Pearson, Fourth Viscount Cowdray, and Marina Rose Cordle, the Viscountess Cowdray. Upon his father’s death, he will become the Fifth Viscount Cowdray and will inherit the Cowdray estate, which sits in West Sussex.

Peregrine Pearson talked about his involvement in managing the sprawling 20,ooo-acre estate in a 2019 interview with Gentleman’s Journal. “We employ 200 people full time, with an extra 150 people in summer. We house 400 families. We employ another 1,000 people through the tenanted businesses on the estate. That all comes with a social responsibility,” he said. “But the dream is to pass it on to my son or daughter in better nick than it’s in now. That’s the mentality when it comes to estates. You’re so grateful that you’ve been given this opportunity, you want others to have the same opportunity that you had.”

He previously dated a Greek royal.

Up until recently, Pearson reportedly dated Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece (who just so happens to be the goddaughter of King Charles III). The Daily Mail reported their split in September 2023.

Dave Benett//Getty Images

Princess Maria-Olympia and Peregrine Pearson

He has a career in property development.

According to Tatler, Pearson works as the director of a property development company in London. He also has a gig as an ambassador of Le Chameau, a footwear company.

His nickname is Perry.

In Pearson’s interview with Gentleman’s Journal, the outlet refers to the Cowdray heir as simply “Perry.”

Peregrine joined Sophie at Paris Fashion Week.

The couple were photographed en route to Louis Vuitton’s after-party following the house’s Fall/Winter 2024 runway show, which closed out Paris Fashion Week. Turner wore an eccentric and colorful look from the brand’s Spring/Summer 2024 collection, consisting of a draping off-the-shoulder top, cream and gray pinstriped high-waist trousers, black suspenders, and black-and-white leather thong sandals. Pearson went casual in an all-black outfit, including a collared shirt and pants.
