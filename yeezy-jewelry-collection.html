Kanye West Yeezy Jewelry Collection

It was just a few weeks ago that Kanye West debuted his Calabasas collection but the rapper/designer is already onto another new Yeezy launch: jewelry.

Three necklaces from the line actually sneakily made their debut last summer on none other than Kim Kardashian at the MTV VMA's but were just revealed as part of the new collection today.

Getty Images

The 12-piece range of necklaces and rings, which was made in collaboration with Jacob Arabo of Jacob & Co., marks West's first foray into jewelry.

Courtesy Yeezy

Yeezy 18k gold necklaces

Inspired by classic Italian art, the romantic necklaces feature pendants engraved with Florentine sculptures and portraits dating back to the 1300s. The pieces start at $1,530 and go upwards of $13,000 for one of the 18k gold necklaces.

Courtesy Yeezy

Yeezy 18k gold rings

The Yeezy jewelry collection is available to shop online now at yeezysupply.com.
