West Duchovny is an American personality and a professional female actor. She gained recognition for playing roles in films like the SyFy series The Magicians, A Mouthful of Air, the Hulu series Saint X, and the Netflix series Painkiller.

Early Life

West Duchovny was born in 1999, making her 24 years old. Her parent’s name is David Duchovny and Téa Leoni, who also gave birth to another child, a son, in 2002. Their parents separated in 2014. West started acting in 2018 and had roles in movies like The X-Files, where she performed alongside her dad.

She went against her mother’s wish to act in the film, and despite this, West had to spend years struggling with the break in the industry. When West told her parents that she had developed a passion for acting, her mother was against this because she did not want her to be in the field.

West Duchovny Career

West had minor roles in series such as The X-Files (alongside her father) and The Magicians, in addition to appearing in films like A Mouthful of Air, The Report, and Linoleum. She had another role in Hulu’s psychological drama Saint X before landing the breakthrough role in another movie, Painkiller, where she portrayed the role of Shannon.
