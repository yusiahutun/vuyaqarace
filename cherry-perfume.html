10 Options That Smell Just Like Tom Ford

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

TikTok has become obsessed with Tom Ford’s Lost Cherry perfume and its sophisticated take on the sweet cherry scent. Even perfume fans that typically despise fruity scents adore its warm and spicy unisex scent profile. The most popular scents of 2024 are genderless, and for a reason. Lost Cherry is proof that a perfume can be both sweet and seductive—and TikTokers have made it clear how much they love the combination.

While you might think of cherry as being overly sweet and youthful, Lost Cherry reinvents the cherry perfume and adds a spicy quality that makes it sexy and mature. With key notes of black cherry, tonka bean, and almond, Lost Cherry smells like cherry liqueur with a touch of bitterness. 

Part of the reason the internet loves Lost Cherry so much is because it seems to perfectly encapsulate one of TikTok’s most beloved aesthetics, the Rockstar Girlfriend. The Rockstar Girlfriend is the cool girl that wears leather jackets and smoky eyeliner. Similar to Tom Ford’s Lost Cherry, the Rockstar Girlfriend aesthetic is sweet and seductive.

While TikTokers have been dying to get their hands on a bottle of Lost Cherry, the price to live out your Rockstar Girlfriend fantasy is pretty steep. (A 30-milliliter bottle costs $250, and a 50-milliliter bottle costs $395.) Thankfully, cherry perfumes like this one have become very popular recently. Of course Tom Ford loyalists would never dare buy a dupe, but rest assured there are tons of cherry perfumes on the market that won’t cost you an arm and a leg.

  • Tom Ford Lost Cherry

    Tom Ford Lost Cherry

    Tom Ford’s Lost Cherry is the perfume that started our cherry obsession. While it’s on the expensive side, it’s at the top of our list and what we’re taking cues from when searching for similar perfumes.

  • Dossier Ambery Cherry

    Dossier Ambery Cherry

    On Sale 10% off

    Ambery Cherry is Dossier’s take on Tom Ford Lost Cherry. With practically identical notes of cherry and almond, this is a great budget friendly option.

  • Fine'ry Not Another Cherry Fragrance Perfume

  • ALT. Cherry Smash

    ALT. Cherry Smash

    ALT. Cherry Smash is also inspired by Lost Cherry and has that same cherry liqueur scent, with notes of cherry and almond. It comes in 30-milliliters, 60-milliliters, and 100-milliliters, and they’re all under $60!

  • The 7 Virtues Cherry Ambition

    The 7 Virtues Cherry Ambition

    Although the 7 Virtues Cherry Ambition is a sweeter cherry scent, it’s very popular on TikTok. This perfume has top notes of cherry, pink pepper, mandarin, and marshmallow along with musk in the base notes.

  • Zara Cherry Smoothie

    Zara Cherry Smoothie

    Sadly, this Zara Cherry Smoothie perfume is out of stock. TikTokers have said it’s a Lost Cherry dupe, as it has notes of cherry, almond, and tonka bean too. Adding this to our list in hopes that it’s restocked soon!

  • Kayali Lovefest Burning Cherry

    Kayali Lovefest Burning Cherry

    For $100, the Kayali Lovefest Burning Cherry is pretty similar to Lost Cherry. Not only is it in the same warm & spicy scent family as Lost Cherry, it’s also described as being a smoky cherry scent, with notes of black cherry, rose damascena, and palo santo.

  • Room 1015 Cherry Punk

    Room 1015 Cherry Punk

    For fans of TikTok’s Rockstar Girlfriend aesthetic, Room 1015 Cherry Punk is for you. The scent is said to be inspired by 1970s London and punk rock style. With notes of cherry, jasmine, saffron, and tonka bean, it’s the ideal scent to wear with your Vivienne Westwood outfit while smoking a cigarette.

  • Brown Girl Jane Dare Cherry Stiletto

    Brown Girl Jane Dare Cherry Stiletto

    Brown Girl Jane Dare Cherry Stiletto has notes of sakura and lychee. If you like cherry scents that are on the sweeter side (but are still seductive), it might be worth the try.

  • Alexandria Fragrances Fructus Virginis

    Alexandria Fragrances Fructus Virginis

    Another perfume taking direct inspiration from Lost Cherry, Fructus Virginis is a long-lasting fragrance with the same notes of cherry, almond, jasmine sambac, and tonka bean.
