Kyle Richards Hosts Bizarre Sance After Lisa Vanderpump's Pup Dies

All in all, Tuesday's "Real Housewives of Beverly Hills" was quite tame, but here's the long and short of it:

After Teddi Mellencamp Arroyave called Dorit Kemsley to apologize for bringing up her old beef with Lisa Rinna, the ladies joined Rinna and her adorable 89-year-old mother, Lois, who was in town visiting from Oregon. (The apology, by the way, was not super well-received.)

Absent from "Lunch with Lois" were Erika Girardi, who was busy making an appearance at Girl Cult, and Lisa Vanderpump, who was distraught over losing one of her pups -- Pink Dog -- unexpectedly.

Later on, Kyle Richards hosted a séance at her house with her longtime psychic, Rebecca. Kyle's one rule for séances: "No electronic cigarettes."

The ladies gathered around Rebecca as she got in touch with Rinna's father, Kyle's mother and Teddi's childhood friend, who died after a horseback riding accident. Rebecca also picked up on Erika's "physic abilities," which Erika confirmed by informing the group of her past life as a Spanish boy who lived in the bottom of a ship. (Does this mean Erika now has three alter-egos?)

Rebecca then looked at Vanderpump and said she felt the presence of Pink Dog. Everyone gasped. Then Rebecca said that she had seen Lisa's tweet about Pink Dog's passing. How convenient.

Although no one really bought into Rebecca's "powers," everyone seemed touched by the experience. At the end of the evening, Erika extended an invitation to all the ladies to join her on an upcoming trip to Berlin. By this point, Erika and Teddi had barely said two words to each other, but Erika made sure to extend the invite to Teddi as well.

Confident that she and Teddi could work toward a friendship following last week's explosive "pretend amnesia" allegation, Erika told Bravo cams, "I'm asking everyone to Berlin, including Teddi, because I always think it's important for a group to stay together."
