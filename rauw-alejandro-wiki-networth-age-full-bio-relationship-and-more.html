Rauw Alejandro, born Raúl Alejandro Ocasio Ruiz, is a renowned rhythm and blues singer, rapper, and lyricist hailing from Puerto Rico. He’s significantly contributed to the Latin music scene, collaborating with esteemed labels like Duars Entertainment and Sony Music Latin. His discography includes “Afrodisíaco” and “Vice Versa.”

Among his hit singles are “Toda,” “Que Le Dé,” “El Efecto,” “Detective,” “Fantasías,” “Tattoo,” and more. He’s worked alongside fellow artists such as Anuel AA, Myke Towers, Ozuna, and Nicky Jam. His achievements have earned him accolades like the iHeartRadio Music Award for Best New Latin Artist in 2021, along with nominations for the Latin Grammy Awards.

With a massive online following, Rauw Alejandro boasts 10.9 million Instagram followers, 10 million YouTube subscribers, 7.6 million Facebook followers, 1.4 million Twitter followers, and 87.5 thousand SoundCloud followers, solidifying his status as a social media sensation.

Rauw Alejandro’s Career

What are the career highlights of Rauw Alejandro?

  • Rauw Alejandro is a popular Puerto Rican singer. Coming from a musical family where both his parents were musicians influenced him to pursue a career in the field. Alejandro has grown up participating in various singing competitions. In 2016, his first and only mixtape Punto de Equilibrio was released. After two years, in 2018, the singer uploaded his hit single Toda.
  • Next year, he released four singles- Que Le Dé, El Efecto, Detective, and Fantasías. His only extended play, Trap Cake, Vol. 1, came out in the same year. Alejandro released his debut album in 2020 with the title Afrodisíaco. Elegí, Ponte Pa’ Mí, and Algo Mágico are the hit singles of the discography. In 2021, his second album, Vice Versa, was released. 2/Catorce and Todo de Ti are the hit singles of this album. Apart from albums, the rapper has organized numerous music tours to date.
