Spanx the Perfect Pants, Wide Leg

The Spanx pants are perfect, so much so they’re definitely deserving of the “Perfect” in their name. They’re made from a high-quality ponte fabric that looks so luxe and feels so comfy. It’s thick, so you can easily wear the bottoms in the wintertime, but not too thick that you’d break a sweat if you wear them in the summer. I reach for them year-round, and especially so when I travel because I can actually sit in them for hours on end without feeling constricted or uncomfortable. 

They’re also incredibly flattering, thanks to the material with hidden butt-lifting powers and tummy control that’s always a welcomed bonus (and a Spanx classic, given the brand is known for its shapewear). They have an easy pull-on silhouette without zippers or buttons that only tend to get in the way, and they’re machine washable, which means no need to dry clean. I’ve worn mine too many times to count, and thus, washed them quite often, but they’ve held up their shape and still fit as good as they did when I first got them. 

So yes, these pants are perfect, and yes, I’m adding yet another pair to my closet while they’re just $84 during Spanx’s year-end sale. Shop the Perfect Pants in three winter-perfect colors before they all sell out, as well as other on-sale bottoms from Spanx's epic savings event.
