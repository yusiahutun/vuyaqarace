Nicola Peltz wears Victoria Beckham's '00s biker jacket in Paris

It looks like Nicola Peltz officially has access to a very covetable wardrobe... While out in Paris this week, the actress was seen wearing a '00s biker jacket previously owned by Victoria Beckham.

Peltz was photographed leaving the Ritz Hotel in the French capital with her husband Brooklyn Beckham – where they are staying ahead of Victoria's latest Paris Fashion Week show – and Noughties style enthusiasts were quick to recognise the blue-and-white striped moto jacket, which previously belonged to her mother-in-law. The cropped leather stye is from Dolce & Gabbana's spring/summer 2001 collection.

Peltz styled her piece of fashion history with high-waisted blue jeans, cat-eye sunglasses, a furry handbag and platform heels, while her husband wore a red sweatshirt from Le Coq Sportif – the French sportswear brand that was also particularly prevalent in the 2000s.

The jacket was originally worn by Victoria back in 2001 – styled with checkerboard jeans and her then-signature aviator sunglasses – to attend a Manchester United football game at Old Trafford.

Alex Livesey//Getty Images

This isn't the first time that Peltz has paid sartorial tribute to her designer mother-in-law; she's regularly been seen wearing pieces designed by Victoria, including the yellow dress that she wore to announce her engagement to Brooklyn back in 2022.

Victoria is preparing to unveil her autumn/winter 2024 collection in Paris this week, joining the roster of brands and fashion houses showing across the Fashion Week schedules in New York, London, Milan and Paris.
