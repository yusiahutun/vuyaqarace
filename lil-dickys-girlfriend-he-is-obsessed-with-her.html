Lil Dicky's Girlfriend: He Is &quot;Obsessed&quot; With Her

Many people have gotten curious about Lil Dicky’s girlfriend because the famous rapper can’t stop gushing about how much he likes her. Lil Dicky has enjoyed great success over the past couple of years in his career. He has released chart-topping tracks like “White Crime,” “Save Dat Money,” “Lemme Freak,” and “Classic Male Pregame.”

He has also made successful forays into the world of acting and television. His comedy series “Dave” is one of the most watched shows, and he showcased his acting skills in it. 

Unfortunately, the same measure of success has not been recorded in his romantic life. This is why, now that he has found the one, the rapper is going all in and giving it all he’s got.

Keep reading to meet Lil Dicky’s girlfriend. She has made him one of the happiest men on planet Earth.

Who Is Lil Dicky’s Girlfriend? 

According to some media sources, Lil Dicky is currently dating a woman named Kristin Batalucco, and she is a producer. The talented rapper and the gorgeous woman have gone Instagram official and look pretty serious. 

Kristin Batalucco is a degree holder from Michigan State University. She has done work for Zerosun Pictures. There, she started as an intern before getting promoted to assistant producer within the company in 2012. Batalucco spent nearly two years at the Denver video agency. Afterward, she started working for HBO in June 2015. 

She worked six months as the company’s Vidosyncrasy and Silicon Valley 3.0 coordinator. Batalucco also worked as a producer at 72 and Sunny for about four years before going freelance. She has now become a senior producer on her own. 

When Did Kristin Batalucco Become Lil Dicky’s Girlfriend? 

Batalucco and Lil Dicky revealed their relationship to the world on Instagram in October 2022. The rapper uploaded pictures of himself and his new beau and wrote in his captions that he is in love. 

The post confirmed the pair are dating, but it did not reveal when their relationship officially started or how it came to be. We know that Lil Dicky is ecstatic with her, and everyone around them is happy for them. 

Will Lil Dicky’s Current Girlfriend Become His Wife?

Before he met his current girlfriend, whom he has claimed to be “obsessed” with, Lil Dicky once revealed that there was a time when finding “the one” was his biggest priority. 

He was a hopeless romantic actively dating and searching for a wife. Unfortunately, he had little luck. The rapper said: “There wasn’t a night where I went out, and my main focus wasn’t ‘Am I going to meet her tonight?'”

Now that he has finally met her, his fans suspect very little is keeping the loved-up rapper from tying the knot with her. 

Is Molly By Lil Dicky a True Story? 

Yes, it is. Lil Dicky released the music video for his track “Molly” about six years ago. The footage saw him play someone attending an ex’s wedding. The song, which featured Brendon Urie, garnered nearly 75 million views and told the story of how Lil Dicky still had feelings for an ex whom he left to pursue music. 

When it was released, his fans were immediately curious about the story and if it was real. One fan asked: 

“In ‘Molly,’ you rap about a girl you still love despite an unfortunate breakup due to a difficult decision regarding your career. Just how hard was it to make that decision, and do you now think it was worth it?” 

He responded: “It really wasn’t that difficult of a decision because I knew what had to be done. I knew that I had to pursue my dreams before anything. Going for music wasn’t something that I regretted, and it wasn’t something that I debated.”

The rapper also said that he and the girl the song was named after and dedicated to had yet to be in a place where they were thinking about marriage. He also admitted that had she been his soulmate, he would not have been able to pick her over choosing to accomplish his dreams. As far as he was concerned, the decision wasn’t difficult. Living with its consequences was. 

If the rapper’s latest relationship continues to head in the right direction, he may soon join the coveted league of rappers like Ice Cube, Snoop Dogg, and Rev Run, who married their soulmates.
