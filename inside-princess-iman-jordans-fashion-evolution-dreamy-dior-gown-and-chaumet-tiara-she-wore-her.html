The ceremony took place on March 12 and was noted by Time magazine as the first royal wedding of 2023.

Those who have been keeping up with this rising royal beauty will have noticed that her style continues to evolve as she emerges into the global spotlight. Naturally, she was a vision on her special day in her white Dior wedding gown. Perhaps she’s taking after her ever-so-chic monarch mum, who also graced the event in a Christian Dior dress and Jennifer Chamandi bag and heels.

So, what are some of the 26-year-old’s best fashion moments to date – and which designers does she appear to favour?

1. Glowing in her Dior wedding gown and Chaumet tiara

Online style watchers went wild for the princess’ white wedding gown from Dior. The timeless piece, which the French fashion house later posted on social media, featured a high illusion neckline and long sleeves cuffed with the same lace.

To finish the mesmerising look, Iman wore a Chaumet tiara crafted in a modern diamond fringe silhouette with a feather design. According to The Court Jeweller, the sparkly piece may be an heirloom from the bride’s grandmother, Princess Muna, but this has not been confirmed by the family.

2. Pre-wedding celebrations in Jordanian-Palestinian designer Reema Dahbour

Iman opted for a floor-length embroidered dress by Jordanian designer Reema Dahbour for her pre-wedding henna ceremony held at Al Husseiniya Palace in Amman in early March.

“Crafting a garment befitting a royal of such grace and elegance has been both a challenging and rewarding experience for our team,” Dahbour wrote on Instagram.

Iman paired the bell-sleeved garment with, as they say, something borrowed, from her mum – a white-and-gold sash belt, which Queen Rania wore on her wedding day back in 1993, per The National News.

3. Tweed trendsetter in a Max Mara coat

The Hashemite blue blood seemed to be channelling her mum’s classic style and sophistication when she wore Max Mara’s “Summer” wool-tweed coat at the National Prayer Breakfast International Luncheon in Washington DC, in February.

As reported by Harper’s Bazaar Arabia, she wore this look at an event where Queen Rania gave a talk about the importance of prayer.

4. Glamming up with a Bottega Veneta slit dress

While out and about with Queen Rania in New York last September, Iman turned heads in her floor-length Bottega Veneta black ruched dress from the brand’s resort 2023 collection. She accessorised the look with the brand’s Knot Minaudière clutch bag and 18-karat silver drop earrings.

The charity gala reportedly aimed to raise funds for organisations committed to addressing gender-based violence.

5. Greek goddess in Zeus+Dione

The Parsons School of Design graduate chose the “Dokos” embroidered linen dress from Greek fashion brand Zeus+Dione in her official portraits following the announcement of her engagement back in July 2022.

Easy, breezy resort-ready chic, the black mini dress exuded Mediterranean vibes and retails for about US$550 on Editorialist.

6. Kicking off 2022 with Roksanda

What a happy family! For the Jordanian royals’ official new year photo to ring in 2022, Princess Iman chose Bottega Veneta once again as she wore the brand’s maroon-coloured button dress, and matched it with a Roksanda copper-plated belt in a similar colour.

7. Matching with mum in white, with her Alexander McQueen skater dress

Wind back a few years and Iman was spotted in France with her mum in 2015 wearing a skater dress by Alexander McQueen.

While her mum’s dress was all white, Iman’s fit-and-flare look incorporated a youthful twist with red colour-blocking at the waistline.

8. A pop of colour with her Louis Vuitton bag

While in Paris that same year, Iman stepped out again in a chic look that her mum would surely approve of. The then-teen strutted out in a simple jeans and tee ensemble with a utilitarian-style white jacket, completing the ‘fit with a bright blue top-handle bag from Louis Vuitton. Named the “Aztec Alma BB Bag”, her limited-edition LV piece injected the right amount of playfulness – and luxury – to her casual-cool look.
