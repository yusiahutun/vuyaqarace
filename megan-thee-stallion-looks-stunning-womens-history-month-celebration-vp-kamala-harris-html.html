Megan Thee Stallion looks stunning at a Women's History Month celebration with VP Kamala Harris

Megan Thee Stallion stunned as she arrived at the Vice President's residence in Washington, DC Saturday to celebrate Women's History Month with an audience of female trailblazers.

The event was an opportunity to discuss economic empowerment of women and focused on paid family leave as an investment in the future.

The 28-year-old super star, who has been showcasing her slimmed down physique, arrived in a classic black and blue floral sweater and matching A-line skirt for the celebration marking Women's History Month.

The Grammy winner shared a series of photos from the gathering on social media, writing simply, 'DC @kamalaharris.' 

She accessorized the look with silver hoop earrings and carried a large black leather purse.

The Hot Girl Summer singer styled her long, dark hair in a braided pony tail.

In Washington: Megan Thee Stallion stunned while attending a celebration marking Women's History Month at the Vice President's residence in Washington, DC Saturday

She wore natural looking makeup with a muted pink lip. 

Megan was invited to the event by Vice President Kamala Harris, who presided over the event.

Harris made history as the first woman and first woman of color to be elected to the vice presidency.

She boasts a multi-ethnic background. Her late mother, Shyamala, immigrated to the US from India. Her father, Donald, 84, is Jamaican.

Speaking to the star studded guest list, Harris said: 'Economic empowerment of women is about an investment in the future of our country.'

'When you lift up the economic status of women, you lift up the economic status of families and communities, and all of society benefits.' 

The Veep looked sleek in a black and white plaid and houndstooth jacket with a sheer black top.

The event was a keystone event for the economic empowerment of women and Harris took a moment to thank everyone for coming to the event in her opening remarks.

Megan rubbed shoulders with Bridgerton actress Simone Ashley, 27.
