Taylor Stonack Job

Taylor Stonack started her career as Marketing Intern at Yelp in 2013. She was working for the company while based in San Diego, California. In 2012, Taylor joined Snooze an A.M Eatery as a corporate trainer/server. She served the company in that role till December 2014.

In 2017, she joined the company as a marketing and community manager. She worked in that role for one year and nine months.

Then, Taylor received the promotion to senior marketing and community manager in May 2019. After quitting her job in the company, she joined Sweep Balayage Bara as a brand development manager. She kept the job until May 2020.

When she was quitting her job at Snooze an A.M Eatery, Taylor was working as a Talent Acquisition Manager in June 2021. Taylor also worked as Skimm’bassador at theSkimm.

From June 2021 to May 2022, Taylor served Alterra Mountain Company as a corporate recruiter. In July 2022, she is working at Melio as Community and Engagement Manager and also at Sweet Potato Social, a company she founded in May 2020.

Years of working and forging relationships with small business owners, entrepreneurs, influencers, and volunteers made her believe that she was solely capable to offer her own skillset and explosive personality on her own. This became the reason why she started her own business.

Her company, Sweet Potato Social is a branding and marketing service whose purpose is to help businesses tell their story, build trust within the community and cultivate lasting and meaningful relationships with their clients.

Talking about academic qualifications, she earned her associates degree in Liberal Arts and Sciences/Liberal Studies from Olympic College. In 2006, she graduated from Western Washington University with a bachelor’s degree in Theatre Arts/Education. She has also volunteered at Share Our Strength and No Kid Hungry.
