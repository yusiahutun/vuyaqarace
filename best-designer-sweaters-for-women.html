15 Best Designer Sweaters 2024

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

Barrie

Best Fitted Designer Sweater

Dior Sweater

Best Fitted Designer Sweater

Dior Sweater

Credit: Dior

This delicately constructed sweater puts a new spin on Dior’s toile de Jouy Sauvage Chiné motif. The ribbed hem, cuff, and neck make for a fitted feel, while the blend of mohair, cashmere, and wool will keep you cozy.

Sizes: 0–14

Materials: 61% cashmere, 20% mohair, 14% polyamide, 5% wool (7-gauge)

Best Cashmere Designer Sweater

Khaite Nalani Sweater

Best Cashmere Designer Sweater

Khaite Nalani Sweater

Credit: Matchesfashion

A delectably cozy knit from Khaite, this striped sweater has an oversize fit that is perfect for cozy nights in, or for dressing up for the office. It’s made with 100 percent cashmere, so you know it’s supple and soft to the touch.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 100% cashmere

Best Wool Designer Sweater

Jil Sander Wool Sweater

Best Wool Designer Sweater

Jil Sander Wool Sweater

Credit: MyTheresa

Infuse some color into your winter wardrobe with this Jil Sander sweater. It has a cropped cut with a crewneck collar, a shape that’s perfect for layering an oversize button-down underneath. Try styling the sweater with high-rise white jeans for a bright outfit.

Sizes: 2–14

Materials: 100% wool

Best Slouchy Designer Sweater

Loewe Anagram Sweater

Best Slouchy Designer Sweater

Loewe Anagram Sweater

Credit: Loewe

Embrace the boldness of Loewe’s signature Anagram with this slouchy loose-weave sweater. The balloon sleeves with cable knit details make a stylish statement, perfect for days you want an outfit that is cozy yet bold.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: Mohair, polyamide

Best Adaptable Designer Sweater

Maria McManus Split Sleeve Collar Sweater

Best Adaptable Designer Sweater

Maria McManus Split Sleeve Collar Sweater

Credit: Maria McManus

Maria McManus’s lusciously soft sweater has one creative detail you might miss. The sleeves are designed with slits along the forearm, to allow you to style the sweater in a number of unique ways (and free your arms if you get too warm). For the pragmatist, this sweater works hard.

Sizes: XS–L

Materials: 50% recycled cashmere, 50% organic cotton

Best White Designer Sweater

Toteme Women’s Wool-Cashmere V-Neck Sweater

Best White Designer Sweater

Toteme Women’s Wool-Cashmere V-Neck Sweater

Credit: Saks

Toteme’s beauty is in its simplicity. This sweater is by no means an exception. The sleek design, in a creamy snow-colored blend of cashmere and wool, is one that requires utmost caution when wearing (no spilled lattes), but it’s worth a little extra care. It’s slightly structured, with a fit that will make you feel dressier just for putting it on.

Sizes: XXS–XL

Materials: 90% wool, 10% cashmere

Best Designer Sweater for a Night Out

Jacquemus La Maille Pau Cardigan

Best Designer Sweater for a Night Out

Jacquemus La Maille Pau Cardigan

Now 12% Off

Credit: Ssense

The night will come when you want to show a little skin, but also find it’s too cold to wear anything but a cozy knit. This Jacquemus sweater is what you need. Strategic buttons are placed diagonally down the front, allowing a little skin to peek out from underneath.

Sizes: XXS–XXL

Materials: 51% alpaca, 25% polyamide, 22% wool, 2% elastane

Best Designer Polo Sweater

Barrie x Sofia Coppola Chunky Cashmere Jumper

Best Designer Polo Sweater

Barrie x Sofia Coppola Chunky Cashmere Jumper

Credit: Barrie

Barrie, the Scottish cashmere brand owned by Chanel, teamed up with filmmaker Sofia Coppola to create this oversize sweater with a sporty collar. It has a chunky, relaxed look, but is oh-so-soft to the touch, thanks to the high-quality cashmere.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 100% cashmere

Best Colorful Designer Sweater

Lisa Yang Aletta Sweater

Best Colorful Designer Sweater

Lisa Yang Aletta Sweater

Credit: MyTheresa

“I love Lisa Yang for good staples with a bit of fashion twist,” says Mytheresa’s Hsu. This bold pink sweater is not for the quiet dresser, but it is ideal for adding a rich touch of color to your outfits. The V-neck collar allows for any tops layered underneath to play as a contrast.

Sizes: 0–2

Materials: 100% cashmere

Best Layered Designer Sweater

Tory Burch Double-Layer Mock-Neck Pullover

Best Layered Designer Sweater

Tory Burch Double-Layer Mock-Neck Pullover

Credit: Tory Burch

Make layering an easy feat with this sweater. It’s designed with double hems and collars to make it look as if you’re wearing two styles at once. When you really can’t be bothered to get dressed, but need something that feels put-together, this sweater will surely do the trick.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 100% merino wool

Best Logo Designer Sweater

Miu Miu Wool Sweater

Best Logo Designer Sweater

Miu Miu Wool Sweater

Credit: Miu Miu

Miu Miu’s logo-ified jacquard sweater is a playful option when something more muted just won’t do. The boxy silhouette can make for a relaxed outfit paired with post-yoga leggings, or can be dressed up with a pleated maxi skirt.

Sizes: 36–46

Materials: Wool

Best Short-Sleeved Designer Sweater

Gucci Cashmere Top With Horsebit

Best Short-Sleeved Designer Sweater

Gucci Cashmere Top With Horsebit

Credit: Gucci

There’s a bit of a throwback touch to this short-sleeved sweater, with Gucci’s signature horsebit detail added to the front as a subtle IYKYK detail. The sweater works year-round, adding a versatile luscious-to-the-touch piece to your wardrobe.

Sizes: XS–3XL

Materials: Cashmere

Best Quiet Luxury Designer Sweater

The Row Ophelia Top in Wool and Cashmere

Best Quiet Luxury Designer Sweater

The Row Ophelia Top in Wool and Cashmere

Credit: The Row

“They are just so irresistible once you touch them, and I prefer oversize shapes for a bit of a statement,” Hsu says of the Row’s sweaters. This oatmeal-hued style is simple, with a relaxed fit you can dress up or down depending on the rest of your outfit. Try heels and a silk skirt, or jeans and a pair of minimalist sneakers.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 65% wool, 35% cashmere

Best Designer Sweater for Warm Weather

Marni Blue & Green Striped Sweater

Best Designer Sweater for Warm Weather

Marni Blue & Green Striped Sweater

Now 23% Off

Credit: Ssense

Consider these the right stripes. Italian label Marni is known for its use of bold colors and fun patterns, and this bright take on stripes is a fun addition to your everyday wardrobe. Since it’s made with 100 percent cotton, this lighter sweater can be worn all the way into summer.

Sizes: XS–L

Materials: 100% cotton

Best Textured Designer Sweater

Stella McCartney Oversize Jacquard-Knit Sweater

Best Textured Designer Sweater

Stella McCartney Oversize Jacquard-Knit Sweater

Credit: Net-a-Porter

Presenting an option with elevated edge: Stella McCartney’s equestrian-themed sweater features a pixelated horse image front and center. It’s designed in a textured alpaca fabric, and has a relaxed, slouchy look to it. Style with sleek complementary pieces, like equestrian boots, to lean into the look.

Sizes: XS–XL

Materials: 75% alpaca, 25% polyamide
