Where is Gemma Owens XXX necklace from on Love Island? All the details

Love Island 2022 feels like a lifetime ago, but thankfully we're still on a high from Love Island 2023.

Fronted by Maya Jama, we loved the new series with Samie, Tom and co. And while last year's IT couple Gemma and Luca have split we're still thinking about Gemma Owen's most unforgettable accessory.

Throughout this series of Love Island, forget the recoupling and drama because all we wanted to know is, where did Gemma Owen get that gorgeous XX chain necklace from?!

Well, it's apparently from Tiffany & Co! Love Island fans and those in the know are almost certain Gemma's is an exclusive vintage piece by Jean Schlumberger for Tiffany in the 80s. (Rumoured to be worth £20,000).
