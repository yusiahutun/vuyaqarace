Naomi Osaka KINL x Walmart Partnership 2023

For sun seekers, beachgoers, and everyone in between, keeping your skin protected against harmful UVA and UVB rays just got a whole lot easier.

Announced today, tennis champion Naomi Osaka's sun- and skincare brand, KINLÒ, is exclusively partnering with Walmart to expand its offerings online and in 2,500 stores nationwide.

"Naomi is truly a pioneer in her work to build a future of skincare that's formulated for melanated skin, while shining a light on the unmet needs affecting people of color," U.S. merchandise director of beauty at Walmart Angel Beasley said in a statement. "Adding the KINLÒ product line to Walmart aligns squarely with our mission to better serve the needs of all of our customers. Walmart is ultra-focused on reflecting our diverse customers and advancing racial equity."

In addition to a retail collaboration, the brand is also introducing new shade selections for its best-selling Golden Rays Sunscreen SPF 50+, which now features light, medium, and deep options to suit a wider range of skin tones.

"I started KINLÒ to educate others about proper sun protection, no matter what they look like or where they live," Osaka said of her line. "Offering KINLÒ products at Walmart is a huge step in bringing suncare awareness and accessibility to a national level."

To complete your face and body skin care rotation, you can also shop a number of new brand SKUs at Walmart, including KINLÒ's tinted and SPF 40 Always Golden Daily Moisturizer, Always Golden Body Lotion, Sunscreen Stick with SPF 30, Cooling Body Gel Moisturizer, and Sun Spot Brightening Moisturizer.

KINLÒ was one of our favorite beauty launches of 2021, making its direct-to-consumer retail debut last September. The brand extended its collection in December with the release of two oils: the Hydrating Facial Oil, which contains a blend of jojoba and olive oils, and KINLÒ's Coconut Body Oil, which was created to use alone or as a carrier oil to dilute essential oils.

"The perfect way to end the day—especially after spending it in the sun—is to restore moisture to your skin," Osaka previously told BAZAAR.com. "I love these two new products because they provide head-to-toe moisture, and are made from all-natural ingredients. Hydration is key for all skin types year round, but it's especially important for melanated skin during the winter months and changing of seasons."

KINLÒ

Osaka has undoubtedly made a name for herself on tennis courts worldwide, and now she's channeling that same star power into her line. KINLÒ—with kin and lò translating to "gold" in Japanese and Haitian Creole, respectively—isn't just another celebrity skincare venture. The range was formulated by Osaka specifically for Black, Brown, and Asian Pacific communities, with the help of top experts, including Dr. Naana Boakye, founder of Bergen Dermatology in Englewood Cliffs, New Jersey.

Each KINLÒ item is priced at less than $15, with the line initially launching with four hero products designed to not only protect your skin from UVA and UVB rays, but also help it recover after being exposed: the reef-safe SPF 50+ broad-spectrum Golden Rays Sunscreen, which features a subtle hint of color; Hydrating Eye Cream; coconut-and-orange-scented Hydrating Golden Mist; and the brand's Hydrating Lip Balm. The products also contain a bevy of skin-soothing natural ingredients, including squalane, niacinamide, aloe vera, and hemp seed oil.

When creating the brand's sunscreen in particular, it was important to Osaka to address common pain points many people of color face—from leftover white residue to a general lack of moisture—to find the right product fit. As an athlete who often plays in direct sunlight, she also considered the mineral sunscreen's longevity when it comes to working up a sweat; this one promises 80 minutes of water resistance and lasts up to two hours before needing to reapply.

“It wasn’t enough to make products that didn’t turn our dark skin white and didn’t have harsh chemicals,” Osaka described in a Women’s Health cover story. “I also wanted to dispel the myth that just because you have dark skin and don’t burn means you don’t need to take care of and protect that skin.” Although summer is on the horizon, utilizing sun protection is a key element of well-rounded skincare routine and something to keep in mind year-round.

You can shop KINLÒ starting April 30 online at walmart.com and kinlo.com.
