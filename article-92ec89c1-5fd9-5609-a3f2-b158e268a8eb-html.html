JUNE 1-3: NEJ KIDS’ FOOTBALL CAMP

Northeast Jones will host a kidsʼ football camp for ages K-7th grade Monday-Wednesday from 8:30-11:30 a.m. each day.

The registration fee for the camp is $60 and includes a snack and drink each day and a camp T-shirt.

Registration for the camp will be on June 1 from 7:30-8:30 a.m. at the NEJ Fieldhouse.

For more information, call NEJ head coach Keith Braddock at 601-803-1234.
