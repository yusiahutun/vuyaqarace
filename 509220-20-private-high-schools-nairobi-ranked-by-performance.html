How many private schools are in Nairobi County?

Which is the most prestigious high school in Kenya?

For public schools, Alliance High School is generally considered the most prestigious. For international schools, some of the most expensive schools, like the International School of Kenya (ISK) and St. Andrews Turi are considered as prestigious due to their resources and reputation.

What is the most expensive private school in Nairobi?

The International School of Kenya is generally considered the most expensive school. Their tuition fees range from $16,974 for kindergarten to $32,543 for Grades 11 and 12, with an additional one-time capital levy of $10,000.
