Hoda Kotb reveals she is ENGAGED to Joel Schiffman

Today host Hoda Kotb has announced that she is engaged to her long-time partner Joel Schiffman, revealing that he popped the question while the couple was enjoying a romantic trip in Mexico this past weekend. 

The 55-year-old shared the joyful news live on the Today show on Monday morning, happily showing off her engagement ring to her co-hosts after revealing that she had a 'secret' to share with them. 

Hoda and asset manager Joel, 61, have been together for more than six years, having celebrated their anniversary in June, and have two adopted children together, Haley Joy, two, and seven-month-old Hope Catherine.

The TV host revealed that Joel popped the question to her while the couple was on vacation and enjoying a romantic dinner on the beach, revealing that he 'said some beautiful things' before getting down on one knee and asking her: 'Will you be my wife.' 

Hoda's announcement left her fellow Today stars in a frenzy of on-air excitement, with Jenna Bush Hager, Craig Melvin, Savannah Guthrie and Al Roker all leaping out of their seats as she shared the surprise news shortly after 8:30am on Monday morning.

'I have to tell you something - a friend of mine, a friend of ours, asked me to let you guys in on a secret,' Hoda started out her announcement. 

'I'll give you her initials, her initials are Hoda Kotb and she's engaged,' the mother-of-two continued while flashing her ring - leaving Today stars open-mouthed in shock. 

'What are you talking about?!' Savannah screamed, while Craig said of her ring: 'When did you put that on? Have you had that on the whole time?!' 

'No, I had it in my bra, I just put it on,' Hoda confessed, before adding: 'Joel proposed in Mexico.' 

'It was a good trip, she's engaged!' Jenna exclaimed, before all of the Today stars began admiring Hoda's beautiful ring - a large radiant cut diamond surrounded by blue stones that look like sapphires.  

Sparkler: Hoda showed off her ring on the show, revealing a radiant cut diamond that is surrounded by blue stones, likely sapphires

Speaking to DailyMail.com, Brilliant Earth engagement ring expert Kathryn Money revealed that Hoda's ring is likely worth between $40,000 and $70,000, judging from the clips shown on Today. 

'Hoda‚Äôs elegant engagement ring appears to feature an estimated 3-4 carat elongated radiant cut diamond surrounded by a halo of sapphires set in a diamond-adorned white gold or platinum band,' the jewelry pro shared. 

'Depending on the quality and specific characteristics of the center gemstone, we estimate the cost of the ring at approximately $40,000-$70,000.'

However while the proposal itself was certainly romantic, Hoda admitted that she was caught completely unawares by her partner, who waited until the very end of the dinner before popping his important question. 

'We went for our usual vacation we ended up having a little dinner on the beach, and I was like... we were done with the dinner and I was eating the churros, like licking the bottom of the bowl, and he was like: "I have something else I'd like to say,"' Hoda shared of the special moment.  

'He said some beautiful things and then he got down on one knee and he said, "Will you be my wife?" And I said, "Yes!"'

When it comes to telling their young daughters, Hoda confessed that the newly-engaged couple has yet to work out exactly how to explain things to them. 

'How are we going to explain, your parents are going to get married?' she said. 'We're still trying to figure out that part of how exactly to explain that to them.' 

Speaking in further detail about the engagement later on in the show, Hoda revealed that Joel had actually been carrying the ring around for a while, waiting for the perfect moment to pop the question. 

'I guess Joel had been carrying the ring around because he was trying to find the right spot,' she shared, revealing that Joel had even considered getting down on one knee and asking her while the pair were on a bowling date at Lucky Strike last week.

'You know what's funny,' she continued. 'He is such a cool customer, so super cool that I literally [had no idea] - because we had discussed it maybe a year or so ago and not really talked about it since...

'So I guess he was carrying the ring around, he brought it with him when we went on a couple-day vacation and I guess he had it tucked away in his pocket and this is how cool he was - we were out, we were having so much fun, we were just having tequila, we were on the beach lounging, I go, "Couldn't you just go to sleep and just forget about everything?"

'And he was like, "Yes but let's just eat dinner and then we'll crash out." No, like zero indication.'

The couple then went to a restaurant at the resort where they had a table set up on the beach - something that Hoda says they do regularly, so her suspicions weren't at all aroused. 

They enjoyed a delicious dinner, and just as Hoda was licking the bottom of the churro bowl, Joel asked to say 'a few things', with the Today host thinking he was going to make 'a cute little toast'.  

The first photo: The couple met at an event in New York City back in 2013 (pictured), where Joel asked Hoda for an autograph

'All of a sudden he got down on one knee and said, "Will you be my wife?"' she shared, before adding: 'He was crying and I was crying, and I looked at him and I thought to myself, "Oh my God, this is that thing."

'The other weird thing is that I felt different from that moment on, which is weird because we have two kids and we've been together for six years, so why would I feel different? I don't know. 

'I feel like it's grounding... I think I've always said "we" but I think I've said it more since? It's weird how that happens, it's like, "We're doing that, we're taking..."'

She confessed that 'even on the flight home' from Mexico, she was looking at Joel as he slept and marveling at the fact that 'we get to do this now'. 

'It's everything,' she added. 'I'm shocked, I'm surprised, but he really is the best man, he is the best man.'

The couple's engagement comes just over five months after they celebrated their six-year anniversary together, with Hoda sharing a sentimental tribute to Joel on her Instagram account the day after the milestone event. 

''Yesterday marked 6 years with this incredible man. 6 years..2 children..2 new jobs...1 grateful heart ps thank you @jolamathisen and #tyler .. if it weren‚Äôt for you two.. we never would have met on that night,' Hoda wrote.

In a 2015 interview with Andy Cohen on Watch What Happens live, the Today host revealed that she and Joel actually first met one another at an event, where Joel asked her to sign an autograph. 

'I said, "Who should I write it to?"' she revealed. 'And he said, "How about to me?" And I said, "How 'bout to you?"'

The couple's first date was then engineered in part by Joanne LaMarca Mathisen, who is now the executive producer on Today with Hoda and Jenna; she revealed earlier this year that it was her husband who convinced Hoda to go to the event in the first place, where she then met Joel, who was a friend of Joanne's.

From there, the couple arranged to go on their first date, and the rest is history. 

'I knew that there was something about him,' Hoda recalled of that night while sharing more details about her engagement on the fourth hour of the Today show, where she and Jenna hosted a joint celebration to mark her engagement and Jenna's 28th birthday. 

After meeting at the event in 2013, Hoda and Joel kept their relationship very much under wraps for the first 18 months, and the Today host only revealed the identity of her then-secret boyfriend in January 2015, having previously referred to him as 'Boots'. 

Since then however, Hoda has been much more open about their romance, with the mother-of-two revealing to DailyMail.com back in March 2018 how supportive her partner was when she shared with him that she wanted to adopt a child. 

The couple adopted Haley Joy, now two, in February 2017, and in April of this year, Hoda and Joel welcomed a second little girl into their family, Hope Catherine. 

'I remember when I went to speak to Joel about it‚Ä¶ my heart was pounding. I think I was afraid of the answer, to be honest. But I knew I had to say something, I had to ask,' she said in a 2018 interview with DailyMail.com, just over a year after they adopted Haley Joy.

'When I went to speak to him, he could tell there was something up, and I think he was just thinking, "What is happening?"'

Eventually, Hoda told him she wanted to pursue adoption with him, and made it clear he should feel free to take time to ponder that possibility. 

'Take a day, a month, a year, whatever you need to think about it, and you can just let me know when you're ready,' she said, recounting the conversation. 'And he just looked at me and said, "I don't need a day."'

Hoda was overwhelmed by Joel's immediate commitment.

'I could have died,' she added. 'I was crying so hard, I soaked his T-shirt. It was incredible.

'I am lucky that I have an incredible life partner who was so supportive and so open to [adoption].'

In a 2018 appearance on Watch What Happens Live with her Today co-host Savannah, Hoda was asked by a viewer whether wedding bells were in the future for the couple, and she admitted that she wasn't sure whether they would ever tie the knot. 

'I love Joel,' she said. 'I'm going to be with Joel until my last breath. I know that.'  

She then said that she and Joel might 'Goldie and Kurt it', referring to the 36-year relationship between Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell, who have never married, despite being together for nearly four decades. 

Speaking on the fourth hour of Today, Hoda once again made that comparison, telling Jenna: 'It's so funny because we always talked about how we were kind of like... we liked the relationship of Kurt Russell and Goldie Hawn. I always thought that was really cool.' 

However, Joel clearly had other ideas.   

The couple currently lives together in Manhattan with their two daughters, however the family spends much of their time on the beach in New Jersey, where they also have a property. 

While Hoda has an early-morning start every Monday through Friday when she anchors Today during the first two hours with Savannah, and then again in the fourth hour with Jenna, Joel works in asset management at Schroders Investment Management.
