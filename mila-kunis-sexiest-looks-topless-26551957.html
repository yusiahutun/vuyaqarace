Despite her obvious good looks, the star admitted in the interview that image is not her priority.

She said: “Image is not a priority for me. A lot of times, people go into this industry with a grandiose idea of fame and think the only way to achieve that is to please everybody.

"Unfortunately, that can lead to very self-conscious on-screen choices. This industry scrutinises you. It’ll tear you apart."
