About

Eric Lloyd is a 37 year old American Actor. Born David Eric Lloyd Morelli on 19th May, 1986 in Glandale, California, USA, he is famous for his roles as Young Kevin Arnold in the ABC coming-of-age comedy series The Wonder Years, Leo Hill in the ABC comedy series Laurie Hill, Johnny in the CBS romantic comedy series Love & War, Charlie Calvin in Disney's The Santa Clause film trilogy and the Disney+ original follow-up Christmas comedy web series The Santa Clauses, Matthew in the 1995 drama film Abandoned and Deceived, "Little John" Warner in the NBC comedy series Jesse, Kyle Grant in the 1996 children's family adventure comedy film Dunston Checks In, and Geeky Lackey in the 2011 slasher film Chromeskull: Laid to Rest 2 in a career that spans 1989–present and 1989–2012. His zodiac sign is Taurus.
