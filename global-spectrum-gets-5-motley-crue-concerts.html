Global Spectrum Gets 5 Motley Crue Concerts

(CelebrityAccess MediaWire) —

Motley Crue will perform at five Global Spectrum-managed facilities during its highly anticipated spring reunion tour — VH1 & VH1 Classic Present "Mötley Crüe: Red White & Crüe Tour 2005…Better Live Than Dead." "Having one of this year's hottest tours in five of our Global Spectrum facilities is a tremendous honor," said John Page, Global Spectrum COO. "The initial response for Motley Crue has been sensational. We are truly looking forward to the return of the Crue at our buildings."

In anticipation of the upcoming performances, Global Spectrum is teaming up with McFarlane Toys to present one on-line contestant in each Global Spectrum city where the band will perform with a limited edition Motley Crue action figure box set and a pair of tickets. There will be five winners in all, who must enter on each venue's website.

"As a company, we want to work with more national tours on unique ways of promoting artists in our venues," added Page. "With the help of McFarlane Toys, this is another terrific opportunity for our facilities to promote their Motley Crue show and generate excitement on their website."

McFarlane Toys' action figure box set release of Vince Neil, Tommy Lee, Nikki Sixx and Mick Mars are featured in the set based off of their Shout at the Devil album. Under a license from Signatures Network, the Mötley Crüe action figure box set, is available in specialty stores and mass retailers. The set comes complete with Lee's elaborate drum set, Neil's microphone and stand, Sixx's bass and Mars' guitar. Learn more at spawn.com.

Motley Crue will perform at the following Global Spectrum-managed arenas:

Wachovia Spectrum Philadelphia, PA March 4

Kemper Arena Kansas City, MO March 15

Veterans Memorial Coliseum Des Moines, IA April 11

John Labatt Centre London, ONT April 26

Budweiser Events Center Loveland, CO May 1

–Bob Grossweiner and Jane Cohen
