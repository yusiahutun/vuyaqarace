Juice WRLD Signs With WME

Juice WRLD. Credit: ASAP Nast.


LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Juice WRLD has signed with WME.

The chart-topping hip-hop artist, whose breakout single “Lucid Dreams” hit No. 2 on Billboard’s Hot 100 last year, will join an A-list roster of artists that includes Drake, Childish Gambino, Kendrick Lamar and others.

Juice WRLD, who was formerly represented by MAC Agency’s Andrew Lieber, will now be represented by a team of agents at WME, according to Billboard. Lieber was reportedly asked by Juice WRLD’s management to join the rapper in the deal, however, he declined to do so as it would have required him to dissolve MAC Agency in the process.

Juice WRLD is managed by Brandon “Lil Bibby” Dickinson, George “G-Money” Dickinson and Pete Jideonwo of Grade A Productions LLC.

The WME signing reportedly took place in December of 2018.
