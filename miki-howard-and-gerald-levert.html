Miki Howard is a 63 year old American Singer. Her zodiac sign is Libra.

American Singer Gerald Levert was born Gerald Edward Levert on 13th July, 1966 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA and passed away on 10th Nov 2006 Cleveland, Ohio, USA aged 40.
