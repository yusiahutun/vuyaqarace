Greta Van Fleet Announce March of The Peaceful Army 2019 World Tour

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) – Greta Van Fleet have announced their “March of the Peaceful Army” World Tour and initial headline dates for 2019. The dates are in support of the band’s highly anticipated debut album, Anthem of the Peaceful Army, which is due out October 19 via Lava/Republic Records.

As part of the itinerary, the band will visit Australia for the first time playing concerts in four cities beginning in Sydney on January 29.  The band will then head over to Europe and the UK in February and March for 12 dates, followed by four weeks across North America that will take them from May into early June.

U.K. Tour Dates:

05 March      Newcastle           O2 Academy
07 March      Glasgow              O2 Academy
08 March      Leeds                  O2 Academy
10 March      Nottingham       Rock City
11 March      Liverpool            Mountford Hall

Tickets for Greta Van Fleet go on-sale to the general public on 19 October 2018 at 10am at www.livenation.co.uk/
