Father's Day Messages 2023 - What to Write in a Father's Day Card

Dads play a big role in our lives, but it's easy for the busyness of life to keep us from telling them how much they mean to us. Enter: Father's Day! Come the third Sunday in June, it's time to buy (or perhaps DIY!) just the right Father's Day gift or plan to treat him to his favorite meal. All of that shows him you care for him, but be sure to tell him too with words themselves too. What to write once you find the perfect card for him though?

Not to fear! We've collected meaningful Father's Day messages to inspire you to write more than just your name. Is he the type who would connect with an inspirational or emotional message? Or are funny dad jokes more his style? What about a meaningful short quote or saying from one of his favorite celebrities? We've got all of those and more below for you to peruse to find just the right thing to say to dear ole dad this year. Whether the message is coming from his daughter or son or his wife, he's sure to treasure every bit of it. And you can keep your message short, even if it's just a few lines, he'll appreciate the sentiment. Here's to fathers and all the ways they shape us for the better!
