Laura Harrier packs on the PDA with rumored flame Sam Jarou in Monaco

It looks like Laura Harrier has a new man in her life following her split from NBA star Klay Thompson.

The model turned actress, 31, was spotted getting cozy during a lunch date with Sam Jarou in Monaco, France, which included a passionate kiss for all to see as they enjoyed food and drinks.

Their afternoon packed full of PDA comes just days after attending the Louis Vuitton Haute-Joaillerie (translates to high jewelry) dinner at La Vigie Restaurant in Monaco on Thursday night.
