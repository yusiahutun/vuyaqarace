Ivanka Trump leaves New York apartment days before husband Jared Kushner takes role

Ivanka Trump looked every bit the First Daughter as she stepped out of her Park Avenue apartment on Friday morning. 

The 35-year-old headed into the weekend in a chic wool coat worn over a white and beige dress, accessorizing her look with $135 nude, pointed-toe pumps from her eponymous fashion line.

Ivanka was strictly business as she walked outside her building in the midst of a conversation on her cellphone. 

In the buff: Ivanka Trump donned a beige wool coat and nude pumps as she headed to work on Friday morning 

Matching: Ivanka had a white and beige striped ensemble underneath her winter coat 

The businesswoman wore sunglasses to protect her eyes from the morning glare, and she used her free arm to carry her a $295 brown bag, which is also from her line.

Ivanka was presumably heading to Trump Tower, where her offices are held. 

Her younger brother Eric, 33, and his wife, Lara, 34, were photographed walking into the lobby of the building before hopping in the elevator together.

Ivanka's older brother Donald Jr. was also seen walking into Trump Tower; however, he appeared to be alone.

In just a week Ivanka's father Donald will be sworn in as the 45th President of the United States, and she and her family are planning a move to Washington, D.C. during his time in office.  

Ivanka's husband is taking on the role of senior adviser for the President-elect, and there is a strong belief that she will be most visible First Daughter in American history. 

The businesswoman recently stepped down from her role as Executive Vice President of Development and Acquisitions at the Trump Organization after 11 years with the company. 

She also sold off all her common stock and resigned from her director position at her fashion line.

These moves seem to suggest that Ivanka will be performing many of the duties assigned to the First Lady in the White House when her father takes office 

Ivanka confirmed her resignation on her Facebook page, explaining that when her father takes office, she will take a 'formal leave of absence' from The Trump Organization and her eponymous apparel and accessories brand.

'I have appointed Abigail Klem as president to lead my brand. She will work with a board of trustees to manage the strategic and day-to-day operations of the business,' she added.

'I am confident that under her leadership the company will continue creating smart, solution-oriented product and content.

Despite her hectic schedule, Ivanka still managed to take her three-year-old son Joseph to school on Thursday.
