Kim Kardashian's Vivienne Westwood Crochet Dress Is So Revealing

  • Kim Kardashian took to Instagram this weekend to share some snaps of her latest revealing dress.
  • The black crochet design is vintage Vivienne Westwood, and features a cut-out neckline and a super short hem.
  • She accessorized the look with tight gladiator heels.

The Kardashian-Jenner sisters regularly entertain us with their surprising and revealing outfits. And this weekend, Kim Kardashian decided to post some photos taken during a night out with her husband, Kanye West.

In the snaps, Kim can be seen lounging in a hotel room, before hitting "London Town" with her beau. Instagram account @kimkwardrobe shared details of Kim's look, revealing that the KKW Beauty founder was wearing a vintage Vivienne Westwood piece from the designer's Autumn Winter '93 collection. Needless to say, it fit Kim perfectly.

Meanwhile, Kim's dramatic René Caovilla Cleo sandals are still available to shop.

Splash News

The makeup mogul also shared photos revealing how she achieved her glam look. She wrote, "I was so inspired working with @nikki_makeup & @lukepluckrose. This look really pushed me to want to try new looks this year! I love meeting super talented artists and getting a chance to work with them after admiring their work on Instagram."

In an interview with BAZAAR.com to promote the launch of SKIMS in Nordstrom, Kim recently revealed, "When I said I wanted seamless shapewear, everyone looked and thought that I was crazy. The thing that always bothered me [with other brands] was that there would be a seam up the front. You would have this really tight dress and then see this seam, defeating the purpose of wearing something that is supposed to look like there is nothing underneath. I’m all about comfort and glamour at the same time. I always think you can do it all, so why not have everything that you need as far as your products?"
