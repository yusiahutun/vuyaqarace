Meghan Markle 'amused' over attention after getting caught by paparazzi

A body language expert has claimed that Meghan Markle was “delighted” to have been snapped by paparazzi.

Meghan Markle was apparently more than happy to have been photographed by paparazzi whilst out with her friend Kelly McKee Zajfen.

Meghan Markle was ‘delighted’ over the paparazzi

Speaking to the Daily Express, body language expert Judi James said the 42-year-old didn’t seem to have the “haughty” body language rituals that A-listers do.

The royal was spotted out in Santa Barbra, California with Kelly, who is a model. The pair went to grab a bite to eat at the Italian restaurant Pane E Vino before shopping at a boutique called Wendy Foster. The two were all smiles and laughing as they enjoyed their time together while ignoring photographers. However, Judi says that Meghan was “amused” by the attention.

Judi said: “Meghan’s body language rituals as she is ‘caught’ by the cameras here is more delighted and generous C-lister than haughty A-lister.”

“Typical A-list body language might involve some gracious poses and B-listers tend to prefer the ‘hide’ option, keeping the head right down and a sombre facial expression as though they are no-way courting publicity or recognition.”

She continued: “Meghan’s friendly smiles suggest she is delighted by the attention, though as she grins with her girlfriend or actually converses with the cameras. For someone so frequently in the public eye and who seemed to be seeking privacy, she looks amused to be getting the recognition here.”
