The Day Trip Festival Returns To Los Angeles For 2024

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) â€” The house and techno-focused Day Trip Festival announced it will take place at the Queen Mary Waterfront in Long Beach for the third year in a row, on June 22-23, 2024.

For 2024, the festival features an expanded lineup along with enhanced festival grounds, including a 25% increase in restroom facilities, and an optimized festival layout to reduce congestion.

Additionally, festival organizers announced a new, expanded stage design, along with the addition of more shade structures to help protect fans from the summer sun.

While the lineup for 2024 has yet to be announced, past editions of the festival featured a a bill that included artists such as John Summit, Fisher, Dom Dolla, Duke Dumont, Eli Brown, Nore en Pure, Meduza, Diplo, Bob Moses, Robin S, and many others.

Tickets for the 21+ Day Trip Festival are available for purchase beginning Thursday, February 29.
