Top 40 Country Songs July 2013

The Top 40 Country Songs for July 2013 don't include a runaway No. 1 hit. In fact, a strong argument could be made for last month's No. 1, 'Boys 'Round Here' by Blake Shelton. While it has peaked on the charts, the laid-back country hit is still among the top sellers and most requested on country radio.

New singles from Hunter Hayes and Luke Bryan also put a bid in for the top country song of July 2013, however. Debuting on the list this month are new singles by Tim McGraw, Kenny Chesney and Miranda Lambert. Chesney's latest, 'When I See This Bar,' checks in higher than the other three, rising to No. 18 in its first month on the list. Other big movers include the new singles from Thomas Rhett, Tyler Farr and Brett Eldredge. After 36 weeks on the charts, 'Don't Ya' has caught fire, cracking our Top 10.

The list of the Top 40 Country Songs for July 2013 is compiled from Billboard chart data and sales figures, in addition to staff opinion and reader feedback.
