About

Azela Robinson is a 58 year old Mexican Actress. Born Azela Jacqueline Robinson Cañedo on 26th August, 1965 in London, England, UK, she is famous for her roles as Dinorah Faberman de Santos in the Televisa telenovela Cañaveral de pasiones, Francisca Rivero de Valdéz in the Televisa telenovela El manantial, Apolonia Rudell de Serrano in the Televisa telenovela Contra viento y marea, Freda Curiel de Ruiz y de Teresa/Juana Felipa Pérez in the Televisa telenovela Llena de amor, Josefa Cabrera in the Televisa telenovela Yo no creo en los hombres, and Marissa in the Televisa biographical musical drama miniseries Por Siempre Joan Sebastian in a career that spans 1990–present.
