Showtime Cancels White Famous Starring Jay Pharoah

Jamie Foxx’s new Showtime comedy starring SNL alum Jay Pharoah will not return for a second season. According to The Hollywood Reporter, the network has officially canceled White Famous, which premiered its debut season in October and wrapped up its 10-episode run last month. Showtime’s other freshman comedy SMILF, meanwhile, recently earned a season 2 order.  Read our interview with Pharoah about the series here.
