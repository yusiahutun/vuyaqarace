Demi Moore Tried the Naked Dress Trend in Sparkly Chain Mail

Demi Moore is a walking reminder that age is just a number because at 61 years young, the actress is wearing the naked dress better than pretty much anyone. Case in point? Over the weekend, Moore attended a party in a fabulously bedazzled and totally sheer fishnet gown.

The actress was spotted in Milan wearing a rhinestone chainmail long-sleeve frock with a high neckline and floor-skimming hem, which she layered over a matching black bra and high-waisted boy shorts set.

The actress let the shimmery dress do the talking and skipped other bling altogether. She did, however, accessorize with her signature pin-straight, butt-length brunette hair, which she styled in a middle part. She also kept her glam simple with rosy cheeks, fringed lashes, and a subtle pink lip.

Moore has never been one to abide by that bogus "dress your age" societal rule. But if you ask us, the age limit does not exist for cutouts, string bikinis, and sheer suits. Earlier this year, Moore proved that sentiment at the 2024 Vanity Fair Oscars after-party in a sultry Versace gown with keyhole cutouts and a built-in choker.

Moore was joined by her three daughters, with ex Bruce Willis, Rumer, Scout, and Tallulah Willis, at the star-studded event and looked more like a fourth sister than mom. The three siblings showed off their inherited fashion sense in gorgeous gowns.
