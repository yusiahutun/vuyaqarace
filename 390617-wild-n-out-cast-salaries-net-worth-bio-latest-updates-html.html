Latest updates on the show: Was Wild 'N Out cancelled?

Nick Cannon has recently received backlash over anti-semitic comments after airing an episode on his podcast show called Cannons Class. After the comments went viral, Cannon was fired by ViacomCBS, the TV company that airs Wild 'N Out show.

While Cannon offered an apology, the question on every fan's mind is, "Is Wild 'N Out cancelled?" The last episode of season 15 of Wild' N Out aired on August 24th, and since the show has a strong following, it's only natural to want to know if there will be a season 16.

When MTV's parent company ViacomCBS ended their relationship with Nick Cannon, he asked for full ownership of the comedy game show series. Furthermore, before he was fired, the show had already been renewed for season 16.

However, there is no official statement from ViacomCBS on whether the American sketch comedy is canceled. For all we know, they might allow the series to continue but under a new host.

Frequently Asked Questions

Want to know more about the fate and cast of Wild N out? Here are answers to some common questions.

Who dies from Wild n Out?

Those wondering which Wild 'n Out cast member dies are referring not to the actual death but the game, Celebrity Death Battle. In this game, one team member rhymes as a celebrity who appears on the screen next to them and engages in a rap battle against the other team. The team with the least points loses.

Who is the white girl on Wild N Out?

Justina Valentine is a famous female cast member in Wild N Out. She has distinct bright red hair and become popular on the show due to her unique freestyling talent.

In fact, Justina was the only female to freestyle on two notable occasions; Sway in the mornings and 5 fingers of death. She joined the show in 2016, and since then, she has been a recurring cast member in every season.
