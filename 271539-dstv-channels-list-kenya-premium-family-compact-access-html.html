How to pay DStv packages in Kenya

You can pay for your packages in a variety of ways. To top up, you can use the MyDStv App, internet banking, USSD, debit order, or visit their walk-in centres or your nearest store.

You can also use the M-Pesa method, which is fast and easy. Below are steps you can follow:

  • Open your M-Pesa app, SIM toolkit or Safaricom app.
  • Select M-Pesa and then the "Lipa na M-Pesa" option.
  • Select "Pay Bill".
  • Enter 444900 as the business number.
  • Enter your DStv SmartCard number as the account number.
  • Enter the amount you want to pay.
  • Enter your Mpesa pin and press "OK".
  • Confirm the details entered. Click "OK"
  • You will then receive a confirmation SMS of your payment.

    • How to upgrade DStv packages in Kenya

    Follow the steps below to upgrade your package.

  • Log in to the app using either your IUC/smartcard number or either your surname or your mobile number.
  • Click on the “Change Package” option on the home page. A list of packages will be presented.
  • Select the desired package, a pop will appear with a "Next" button.
  • Click on "Next", and you will be directed to the "Change Package" screen, with the payment summary with what needs to be paid.
  • Click on "Pay Now" to continue to the "Make a Payment" page.
