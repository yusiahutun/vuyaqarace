Eddie Cibrian sells home he shared with ex-wife Brandi Glanville for $635k

Actor Eddie Cibrian has sold a townhouse he shared with ex-wife Brandi Glanville.

The 43-year-old found a buyer for his three-bedroom property in Toluca Lake, Los Angeles after lowering the price to $635,000, reports TMZ.

He originally listed the 1800-square foot home for an extra $100,000 earlier this year, before reducing the price, the website reports.
