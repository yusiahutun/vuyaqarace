10 scarf coats to shop if you're obsessed with Meghan Markles latest look

The scarf coat is the outerwear trend dominating the street style set, and earlier this month Meghan Markle was the latest celebrity to wear the coveted style as she stepped out for dinner in Los Angeles with Prince Harry.

The 41-year-old Duchess embodied minimalist chic in her beige Carolina Herrera scarf-coat hybrid, which she paired with cropped black leather trousers, Dior pumps, a Dior Pouchette clutch bag and diamond hoop earrings.
