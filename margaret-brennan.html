Margaret Brennan- Professional Career

Margaret Brennan is a Washington, D.C.-based journalist. She moderates CBS News’ Face the Nation with Margaret Brennan. Brennan also serves as the network’s chief international affairs correspondent. She formerly served as a White House correspondent for this network, and she has been covering Washington since 2012.

Before joining CBS, this journalist hosted the daily show InBusiness with Margaret Brennan. This program was broadcast on Bloomberg Television. This journalist, too, worked as a CNBC correspondent and contributed to NBC News programming.

This writer belongs to the Gridiron Club as well as the Council on Foreign Relations. The journalist serves on the alumni advisory board of the University of Virginia School. In addition, this author serves on the Advisory Board of the Smurfit School of Business at University College Dublin.
