Daniela Bobadilla Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Daniela Bobadilla is a Canadian actress born in Mexico. She is best known for her role as Sam Goodson in the FX series Anger Management and series The Middle. Apart from this, she has also been a voice actor, in 2017 did a voice-over in animated comedy Ferdinand, followed by her another voice role in the animated superhero film Justice League vs. the Fatal Five as Miss Martian.

Early life

Daniela Bobadilla was born on April 4, 1993, in Mexico City, Mexico to father Carlos and mother Haydee Bobadilla. She has a younger sister. When she was 6 years old, her family relocated to North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. At a very early age, Daniela showed an aptitude for performing and acting, her family was her first acting audience while she was growing up. Then later when Bobadilla was in secondary school, she finally began auditioning for roles.

Daniela attended middle school at Summit Middle School in Coquitlam. Later she went to attended Heritage Woods Secondary School in Port Moody. Side by side, Daniela also learned acting at the Theatrix Youththeatre Society and subsequently performed at various school plays. She even participated in inter-school competitions.

Daniela Bobadilla Career

In 2008, Bobadilla began her acting career, after auditioning for many roles, she ended up on the sets of The CW network for recurring roles in series. She starred in Smallville and Supernatural. Then as she decided to take her acting career further, Daniela relocated to Los Angeles and made her first debut in the American movie Lies in Plain Sight as a 13-year-old blind adolescent. She also played Jenny McCaffrey in the television film Oliver’s Ghost.

She made her debut in the film industry with a short film, Tomorrow Maybe as Jessica in 2012, and the same year she was also seen in Rosita Lopez for President. Her major career breakthrough was seen when Daniela was cast as Sam Goodson, the daughter of Charlie Sheen’s character on sitcom Anger Management in 2012. She was seen as one of the lead roles and stayed with the series till 2014.

Bobadilla was seen on the sets of the Lifetime film, Perfect High as a primary character in 2015 opposite Bella Throne. She also featured on popular series such as Desperate Housewives, Awake, Modern Family, The Night Shift, and many more. In 2016, she appeared as Lexie Brooks in the American sitcom The Middle where she stayed with the crew till 2018. The same year, in 2016, Daniela appeared in Face 2 Face, a teen drama film as Madison Daniels.

Social media

Daniela connects with her fans through various social media networking handles such as Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook. She is a verified user on Instagram and Twitter with 51k followers and 18.3k followers respectively. Daniela also has a lifestyle account under the username @lessonslearned on Instagram.
