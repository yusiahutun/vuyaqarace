Diamond Introduces Ex-Girlfriend Who Dumped Him Before Stardom: &quot;Alifanya Niandike Kamwambie&quot;

  • Wasafi Classic Baby (WCB) boss Diamond Platnumz surprised fans by inviting his ex-girlfriend Sarah onstage during a concert
  • The Tanzanian crooner opened up about how Sarah inspired his hit song Kamwambie and how much he loved her
  • The former couple warmly embraced each other on stage and Diamond explained that Sarah had moved on

Tanzanian singer Diamond Platnumz has introduced his ex-girlfriend, Sarah.

The celebrated artiste invited Sarah to the stage during his show and shared details about their past relationship with his fans.

What did Diamond say about ex Sarah?

According to Diamond, their romantic history dates back 15 years, and Sarah served as the muse for his hit song Kamwambie, which catapulted him to stardom.

Despite their love, their paths diverged, and Sarah has since moved on, now having a family of her own.

Diamond emotionally expressed:

"I wrote this song from a deeply personal experience. There’s a lady I hold dear, and I saw her here; her name is Sarah. She is the very reason I sang ‘Kamwambie’.”

Diamond, Sarah tightly hug each other

As Sarah stepped onto the stage, she bashfully embraced the superstar, her hand partially concealing her face.

Diamond reminisced about their youth, describing how he once regarded her as a Beyoncé-like figure, driving him to the brink of infatuation.

"At the time she was young. She is shy, you know she has a family right now, this is Sarah. I used to love her so much. At the time, I used to look at her like she was Beyoncé; she drove me crazy. She character developed me, I wrote this because of her," the singer said.

Zuchu subtly calls out Diamond for using her

In a separate story, Zuchu recently put Diamond on the spot, demanding to know when the WCB boss intends to marry her.

While at a concert, the Sukari hitmaker, who was performing with her boyfriend Diamond, asked him when he intended to marry her or if he was only using her.

Diamond then laughed as the crowd cheered. Then Zuchu told the crowd to shout 'marry her' in unison.
