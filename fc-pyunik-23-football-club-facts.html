Founded in 1992

FC Pyunik was established in 1992, following the dissolution of the Soviet Union and the emergence of an independent Armenia. Since its inception, the club has become synonymous with success and has become a dominant force in Armenian football.

Home at the Vazgen Sargsyan Republican Stadium

The team proudly calls the Vazgen Sargsyan Republican Stadium, located in the capital city of Yerevan, its home. This iconic stadium has witnessed some of FC Pyunik’s greatest victories and is a symbol of the club’s strong presence in the country.

Record-Breaking Title Wins

FC Pyunik holds the record for the most Armenian Premier League titles, having clinched the championship an impressive 15 times. This remarkable achievement showcases the club’s dominance in domestic competitions.
