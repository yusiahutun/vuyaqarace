Renee Zellweger being engaged to TV presenter would make Bridget Jones proud

Renee Zellweger is reportedly “engaged” to British TV presenter Ant Anstead after a two-year relationship, which the couple has kept away from the media spotlight. The news would certainly make Renee’s British character Bridget Jones very proud.

The Oscar-winning actress might be one step close to walking down the aisle after reports about her “engagement” with partner Ant Anstead. While Renee and Ant have kept their relationship low-key, the British TV host has given an insight into their romance with several loved-up pictures on Instagram.

Renee Zellweger and Ant Anstead “engaged”

Renee and Ant are reportedly “engaged” after dating for two years. The couple met on the set of the reality series Celebrity IOU: Joyride in 2021 where Ant transformed classic cars.

A source close to the pair told The Sun: “Renee and Ant are keeping their plans incredibly private, they’re not planning a big public announcement. She has been telling her inner circle about organising their nuptials, everything will be very low-key.”

The insider added that their relationship “is super strong” and “they’re madly in love, and they can’t wait to get hitched”.

Couple got together for Goodwood Festival of Speed event

Now that Renee and Ant are stronger than ever in their relationship, we hope that the celebrity couple blesses their fans with more loved-up snaps on social media.

The Wheeler Dealers host shared an adorable family snap with his two children, Archie, 16, and Amelie, 19, from a black-tie event at the 2023 Goodwood Festival of Speed, on Instagram.

The TV star sported a classic black suit, Renee an elegant gold gown, while Ant’s teenage son Archie sported a similar suit and Amelie stunned with a long black dress with golden details.

The family affair was for the annual motorsports event which takes place in Goodwood, West Sussex close to the time of the British Grand Prix. Ant also appeared on stage during one of the festival’s days in collaboration with Ebay UK.

Ant and Renee’s relationship

Ant and the Hollywood actress met during the filming of the Discovery Channel show Celebrity IOU: Joyride two years ago.

The Bridget Jones’s Diary movie star appeared on the reality show to honor her late friend and publicist Nanci Ryder. Renee oversaw the transformation of a car that was gifted to Nanci’s nurses who took care of her before her passing.

Meanwhile, Ant’s latest post with Renee was filled with messages of love from their fans. “Couldn’t be happier for you man!” one comment read. “Such an awesome team.”

“Renee looks amazing and you two are the cutest couple! Adorable children,” another fan commented. “I wish the best for you..you seem extremely happy! Good for you!”
