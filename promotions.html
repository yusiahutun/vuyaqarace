Discounts And Promotions Laurel Mercantile

Home / Discounts And Promotions

Laurel Mercantile retains the right to change, limit or cancel any discount code at any time without notice.

 

Unless otherwise noted, any discount or promo code offered by Laurel Mercantile Co. applies only to products in the linked collection. Offers cannot be redeemed for cash or combined with any other promotion. Discount codes cannot be applied to purchases before the code becomes active or after they become inactive. Discount codes cannot be applied to custom orders, oversized items such as counter tops/islands or drop ship items unless stated. Discount codes apply to in-stock items only and cannot be applied to future purchases. 

 
Discount codes do not apply to, and are not limited to: Scotsman Co. Islands and Counter Tops, Custom House Portraits, frame art, drop ship art, furniture, custom orders, gift cards, laundry canvas trucks, rugs, gift items, some already discounted products, etc.

 

Discount codes are not stackable.

 

Military, first responder and teacher discounts

Laurel Mercantile Co. partners with GovX to provide discounts to military, first responders and teachers. To receive this discount, you must first sign-up with and be verified by GovX. Once your account is created, you can use this to receive a discount on any order that does not have another discount code applied. To create a GovX account, click the "GovX Military, first responder, and teacher discount" box at the bottom of your cart before checkout. You will then be led through the sign-in or sign-up process to receive your discount. 

Military, first responder, and teacher discount are not stackable with other discount codes.
