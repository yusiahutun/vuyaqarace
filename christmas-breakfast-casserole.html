Christmas Breakfast Casserole: 11 Easy and Delicious Recipes

Christmas morning is full of magic. And once all those presents under the tree are unwrapped, we love gathering everyone around the table for a holiday-special breakfast. One of our favorite things to serve? Casserole! They’re perfect for a crowd and often you can prep them in advance, leaving you more time to spend with loved ones. Here, we’ve gathered some of our tried-and-true Christmas breakfast casserole recipes that let you wow without the work. They’re so easy and delicious, we’re sure they’ll become a part of your holiday traditions year after year. Merry Christmas!

11 easy Christmas breakfast casserole recipes

From sweet to savory, we’ve got a casserole that’s sure to please. And since they come together so fast, you can pick a few of your favorites to serve this Christmas.
