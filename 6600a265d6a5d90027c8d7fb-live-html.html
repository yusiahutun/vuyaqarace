USA vs Mexico LIVE updates from Nations League Final: Final score and highlights

The USMNT and Mexico will face off on Sunday night in the CONCACAF Nations League final, in what will be the 76th meeting in their contentious history. USA defeated Jamaica in the semi-finals, while Mexico crushed Panama.

This will be a rematch of the 2021 CONCACAF Nations League final, in which the USMNT won the inaugural tournament in a dramatic match that went to extra time. The two sides also met in 2023, with the U.S. winning 3-0 in the semi-finals.

The game will be played at AT&T Stadium in Arlington, Texas and is expected to kick off at 9:15pm ET / 6:15pm PT. It will be shown on Paramount+ and Univision.

USA vs Mexico head-to-head history

This will be the 76th meeting between the two North American nations in their history. Mexico have a large edge in the all-time series, winning 36 meetings and losing 17 of them. But since 2000, it's been a completely different story.

Since the turn of the century, the USMNT have a 18-9-8 series lead. They are also on a six-game unbeaten streak against Mexico, winning four of those matchups. It's the longest unbeaten run against Mexico since 2015.
