Katrina Jackson’s Bio, Wiki, Parents

Katrina Jackson was born on 17th May 1991 in Chicago, Illinois to Ken and Kathleen Jackson. She grew up with her two brothers namely; Kenny Jackson and James Jackson. Jackson is of American nationality and part of mixed ethnicity. She was born under the birth sign of Taurus.

Regarding her education, Jackson attended the University of Missouri to achieve her bachelor’s degree but dropped out to pursue a career in arts.

Jackson started her career while she was in university. She began her career by tattooing and originally became an expert when she originated tattooing in her mother’s basement and her father’s kitchen. Jackson’s talent was appreciated and acknowledged when she joined the 9Mag studio and also appeared on the Spin-Off reality series Black Ink Crew: Chicago alongside Ryan Henry, the owner of 9Mag.
