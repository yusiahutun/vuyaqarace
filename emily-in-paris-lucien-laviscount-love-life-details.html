    The 29-year-old British star has been linked to a number of A-list stars over the years. Early on in his career, following stints on British shows such as and , he had brief flings with star Dominique Jackson, his co-star Chelsee Healey and his on-screen girlfriend in , Brooke Vincent.

    Lucien turned his sights to Hollywood a few years later, and after a small part in Supernatural managed to land a recurring role in the series . Rumours began to swirl around the time that he was dating his co-star Keke Palmer after the two of them were seen on several dates around LA.
